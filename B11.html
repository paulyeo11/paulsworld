Be transformed by beholding Christ — live by the Spirit, not by the tree of knowledge.

🌅

Morning Nourishment

2 Cor. 3:18

But we all with unveiled face, beholding and reflecting like a mirror the glory of the Lord, are being transformed into the same image from glory to glory, even as from the Lord Spirit.

Gal. 5:25

If we live by the Spirit, let us also walk by the Spirit.

Job had everything — wealth, health, integrity, a good reputation. He was morally excellent. But God's assessment was sobering: Job had nothing of God. He was content in his own goodness, his own success, his own ethical achievement. God had to strip all of it away.

God's intention was not to punish Job — it was to usher him into a deeper realm. A realm where he would stop gaining things and blessings, and start gaining God Himself. God ushered Job from the realm of good and evil — from human morality — into the realm of the life-giving Spirit.

Before Satan's attack, Job was satisfied. Afterwards, he was empty — but empty is exactly what God needed him to be. Only an empty vessel can be filled. God was not destroying Job; He was making room for Himself.

📖 Today's Reading

Today's Triune God is consummated in the Spirit. The Spirit is not just one of three — He is the aggregate, the fullness, of the Father, Son and Spirit together. When we receive the Spirit, we receive all three. He is the all-inclusive, life-giving Spirit living within us right now.

We are to do everything by the Spirit — pray by the Spirit, read the Bible by the Spirit, love others by the Spirit, speak by the Spirit, even preach the gospel by the Spirit. This is not religion. This is the mingling of the processed Triune God with the regenerated human spirit.

In our daily life we must choose: the tree of knowledge — doing what seems right to us, relying on our own judgment, moral effort, and human wisdom — or the tree of life — doing everything by exercising our spirit and letting the Spirit live through us. The consummation of this is not a good life. It is the New Jerusalem.

Lord, empty me of myself. Fill me with Your Spirit. Let me do everything by the Spirit today — not by my own effort or judgment. Transform me from glory to glory.

Tree of life, not tree of knowledge

Stop living by what seems good or right to you. Live by the Spirit — do everything by exercising your spirit. That is the tree of life.

🏙️

Every step lived by the Spirit is a step toward the ultimate destination — the New Jerusalem. Your daily walk by the Spirit is building something eternal.
