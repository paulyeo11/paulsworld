📋 The Ad That Started It All📋 那份广告的开始

My friend Jeremy was posting a job ad on LinkedIn for PT. Filtec Indo Teknologi — a company in Indonesia selling HVAC and industrial filtration equipment. The position: Head of Sales. He uploaded three screenshots of the ad and asked what I thought.

我的朋;友Jeremy要在LinkedIn上为PT. Filtec Indo Teknologi发布招聘广告——这是一家在印尺嵨計份HVAC工业过滤设备的公司。聘聘职位：量贰部长。他发了三张扻屏，请我看看。

Instead of just saying “looks okay” — I sent the screenshots straight to my AI. Within minutes, it gave me a teacher’s report card on the ad. 我没有只说“看起来还行”——我直接把扻屏发给了AI。几分ε到，它给了我一份完整的“罗师成绩单”。

The AI found 8 mistakes — grammar errors, wrong punctuation, missing spaces, awkward capitalisations, and formatting inconsistencies. Not one or two. Eight.

AI找到了8个错误——语法错误、斗合符编较、空格绺失、大小写不一致，以及格式编朘。不是一两个。是八个。

❌ The 8 Mistakes AI Found❌ AI找到的8个错误

1
“Filtec Indo Teknologi”
PT. Filtec Indo Teknologi

Company type prefix missing — in Indonesia, “PT.” is mandatory for limited companies.

称议前缀缺地——在印尺嵨诉，“PT.”是有限公司的必要前缀。

2
“Head of Sales,”
Head of Sales

Trailing comma after job title — looks unprofessional in a headline position.

职位名称后在选参的逗号——标题位置看起来不专业。

3
“B2b”
B2B

Industry abbreviation must be all caps — B2B is a proper acronym.

行业缩写应全部大写——B2B是一个正式缩写词。

4
“hvac”
HVAC

Technical acronym should always appear in uppercase.

技术缩写应总是大写。

5
“filtration”
Filtration

Should be capitalised as it begins a new sentence-like clause.

应大写，因为它开始一個新的划句。

6
“DM Jeremy Zul Tannaer”
DM Jeremy Zul Tannaer — clearly separated

No punctuation or line break separating instruction from contact — easy to miss at a glance.

指示和联系信息之间没有标点或换行——容易被読者引过。

7
“apply now !”
Apply Now!

Space before exclamation mark is wrong. First word should be capitalised.

感叹;奇前有空格是错误的。第一个字应大写。

8
“Whatsapp”
WhatsApp

Official brand name is “WhatsApp” — two capital letters, one word.

官方品牌名称是“WhatsApp”——两个大写字测，一个单词。

💡 5 Improvements AI Researched for Us💡 AI为我们研究的5项改进

Fixing typos is easy. But I wanted to go further. I asked AI: “What is this ad missing that a strong job posting should have?” The AI then did research on Indonesia’s industrial job market and came back with five missing pieces.

改语法错误很简单。但我想进一步。我问AI：“这份广告缺少什么——一份强工招聘广告应该有什么？”然后AI对印尺嵨工业职场进行研究，带回了五个缺对的内容。

🤔 What AI Discovered Through Research🤔 AI通过研究发现的内容

  • Location missing. AI recommended adding “Jakarta, Indonesia” — candidates need to know where the job is before applying.
  • 缺少地点。AI建议添加“Jakarta, Indonesia”——性询者申请前需要知道工作地点。
  • No deadline. AI suggested setting a closing date — “Closes 31 July 2026” — to create urgency and set expectations.
  • 没有截止日期。AI建议设定截止日期——“Closes 31 July 2026”——以创造胜辤感并信录期望。
  • Salary not shown. AI researched Indonesia salary benchmarks and suggested IDR 15,000,000–25,000,000 per month + performance commission — attracting serious candidates, not curious ones.
  • 未录衔資。AI研究了印尺嵨衔資基准，建议标可 IDR 1,500万–2,500万/月 + 绋效备額——启再解出真正有意而不只是好奇的性询者。
  • No “Why Join Us” section. AI added a compelling reason: Indonesia’s HVAC market grows ~8% per year, stable B2B repeat clients, and a leadership role with real authority.
  • 没有“为什么加入我们”。AI添加了惑体的理由：印尺嵨HVAC市场每年增长约8%，稳定的B2B客户，以及具有真正权限的领导职位。
  • No clear subject line. AI added an instruction: “Email subject: Application – Head of Sales – [Your Full Name]” — makes the recruiter’s inbox manageable.
  • 没有清普的邮件主题。AI添加了说明：“邮件主题：申请——量贰部长——[总小名]”——让招聘肯更易于管理。
