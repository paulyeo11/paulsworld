Business Overview业务概览

Nvidia is the leading designer of GPUs and AI accelerators — the "picks and shovels" of the AI boom. Revenue is now dominated by the data-center segment:英伟达是GPU与AI加速器的龙头设计商 — AI热潮中的“卖铲人”。收入如今主要来自数据中心板块：

🖥️ Data Center数据中心
AI training/inference GPUs (H100/H200/Blackwell), networking (InfiniBand, NVLink) — by far the biggest and fastest-growing segment.AI训练/推理GPU（H100/H200/Blackwell）、网络（InfiniBand、NVLink） — 目前规模最大、增长最快的板块。
🎮 Gaming游戏
GeForce RTX graphics cards for PC gaming — the original core business, still meaningful but now a minority of revenue.面向PC游戏的GeForce RTX显卡 — 最初的核心业务，仍有分量，但如今只占收入的一小部分。
🚗 Auto, Pro-Viz & Software汽车、专业可视化与软件
Self-driving platforms (DRIVE), professional visualisation (RTX/Omniverse), and the CUDA software moat that locks developers in.自动驾驶平台（DRIVE）、专业可视化（RTX/Omniverse），以及锁定开发者的CUDA软件护城河。

Why It's on My Watchlist为何在我的观察清单上

  • The default AI chip — nearly every large AI model is trained on Nvidia hardware, giving it enormous pricing power right now.AI芯片的默认选择 — 几乎所有大型AI模型都在英伟达硬件上训练，使其目前拥有极强的定价能力。
  • CUDA software moat — years of tooling and libraries make it hard for developers to switch to a rival chip.CUDA软件护城河 — 多年积累的工具与库让开发者难以转向竞争对手的芯片。
  • Explosive data-center growth — cloud providers keep placing multi-billion-dollar orders for the next GPU generation.数据中心业务爆发式增长 — 云厂商持续下达数十亿美元的下一代GPU订单。
  • High volatility — big swings around earnings mean wider option premiums, which matters for Iron Condor timing (see T21).高波动性 — 财报前后大幅波动意味着更高的期权权利金，这对铁鹰策略的时机很重要（见T21）。
