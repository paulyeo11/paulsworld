📖 What Is This, Again?这到底是什么？

Astrea bonds (from Astrea IV through Astrea 8) are retail bonds issued out of Singapore, backed by cash flows from a portfolio of private-equity funds rather than by a single company. They're sold in board lots on SGX and pay a fixed coupon, usually with an "optional redemption" (call) date years before the final legal maturity.

Astrea系列债券（从Astrea IV到Astrea 8）是新加坡发行的零售债券，由一篮子私募股权基金的现金流作为担保，而非单一公司信用。它们在新加坡交易所以整手交易，支付固定票息，通常在最终到期日之前设有"可选赎回日"（提前赎回日）。

💰 How to Sell It如何卖出

  1. Open your brokerage app (the one that holds this bond — e.g. Tiger, IBKR, POEMS, DBS Vickers) and find the bond under your portfolio holdings, not the stock list.打开你的券商App（持有该债券的那个，如老虎证券、IBKR、POEMS、DBS Vickers），在"持仓"里找到该债券，而不是股票列表。
  2. Check the current bid price and yield. Astrea bonds can be thinly traded, so the bid may sit below par (100) — compare it to what you paid.查看当前买盘价和收益率。Astrea债券成交量可能不大，买盘价可能低于面值（100）——与你的买入价比较。
  3. Check the next optional redemption (call) date. If it's close, the issuer may redeem the whole tranche at par soon — sometimes it's better to just wait than sell at a discount now.查看下一个可选赎回日。如果日期临近，发行方可能很快按面值赎回整批债券——有时候等待赎回比现在折价卖出更划算。
  4. If you still want to sell now, place a limit sell order (not a market order) at or near the current bid, in board-lot size, through the brokerage app.如果仍想现在卖出，通过券商App以整手为单位，按接近当前买盘价下限价卖单（不要用市价单）。
  5. Settlement is typically T+2. Once it clears, proceeds land in your brokerage cash account — you can then withdraw to your bank as usual.结算通常为T+2（交易后两个工作日）。到账后，款项会进入你的券商现金账户，之后可照常提款到银行。
  6. If you're unsure which class (A-1, A-2, B, C etc.) or CUSIP/ISIN you hold, call your broker's helpline and quote the bond name shown in your portfolio — they can confirm the exact code before you sell.如果不确定持有的是哪一级（A-1、A-2、B、C等）或具体代码，可致电券商客服，报出持仓中显示的债券名称，请他们在卖出前帮你确认。

💡 Sell vs. Hold to Call卖出 vs. 等待赎回

Selling now locks in whatever the market bid is today. Holding until the optional redemption date usually gets you par (100) plus accrued interest — often the better outcome if that date isn't far off. Check the date before you rush to sell.

现在卖出，锁定的是当前市场买盘价。持有到可选赎回日通常能拿回面值（100）加应计利息——如果赎回日不远，往往是更好的选择。卖出前先查一下日期。

⚠️ Before You Act行动前请注意

  • Bond prices move with interest rates and credit sentiment — the bid today may differ from last time you checked.
  • 债券价格会随利率和市场信心波动——今天的买盘价可能与你上次查看时不同。
  • Confirm there are no early-redemption notices already issued for your tranche — check your broker's notifications or the SGX announcement page.
  • 确认你持有的分级没有已发出的提前赎回通知——可查看券商通知或新交所公告页面。
  • This is a reminder and general process guide only — not a recommendation to buy, sell, or hold any specific bond.
  • 这只是提醒和一般流程指南——并非对任何具体债券买入、卖出或持有的建议。
