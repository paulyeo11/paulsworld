Grace is God Himself — and you are becoming a divine building, mingled with Him.

🌅

Morning Nourishment

Heb. 4:16

Let us therefore come forward with boldness to the throne of grace that we may receive mercy and find grace for timely help.

Heb. 11:7

By faith Noah, having been divinely instructed concerning things not yet seen, prepared an ark for the salvation of his house, through which he condemned the world, and became heir of the righteousness which is according to faith.

Noah found grace — and grace is not a thing God gives. Grace is God Himself. Grace is God coming to us, being enjoyed by us, and helping us face the reality of our flesh. If it were not for the flesh, God would not need to give us so much grace.

The flesh is the very presence of the devil. But grace is the very presence of God. Every minute — even while you are praying — Satan is in your flesh trying to frustrate you. But God is greater than Satan. When God stands with you, that is grace.

We cannot do anything about the flesh on our own. But there is somewhere we can go — the throne of grace. We come boldly and find mercy and timely help for every single moment of weakness.

📖 Today's Reading

In God's creation, He worked with His hands — but nothing of Himself was put into His creatures. In God's building, He works with Himself as the material. God's building is a divine mingling of Himself with man.

The first building of God in Scripture is Noah's ark. The ark is a type of Christ — the Redeemer and Savior — but also a building. Christ is not only God; He is the God-man — God built together with man. John 1:1 says the Word was God. John 1:14 says the Word became flesh and tabernacled among us. This incarnation was God mingling Himself with humanity — a building.

Before we were saved, we were only a creation. Now that we are saved, God has mingled Himself with us. He has mingled His divine nature with our human nature, making us a divine building. You are no longer merely a creature — you are a building of God.

Lord, I cannot handle the flesh on my own. I come boldly to Your throne of grace. Stand with me. Be my strength today.

Big Idea: Grace is God Himself — and you are becoming a divine building, mingled with Him.
1
🙏
Grace is God Himself coming to you

Grace is not a gift package God sends. Grace is God Himself showing up — standing with you, being your strength, facing your weakness with you.

2
💪
The flesh makes you run to God

The flesh is uncomfortable — but it is also the thing that drives you to the throne of grace. Your weakness is not a failure; it is your invitation to God's presence.

3
🏛️
God wants to build Himself into you

Creation = God working with His hands. Building = God working with Himself as the material. You are not just God's creature — you are becoming His building.

4
⛵
The ark = Christ the God-man

Noah's ark is a picture of Christ — God and man built together. Jesus is the meeting point of divinity and humanity. In Him, two natures are mingled as one.

5
✨
You are now a divine building

Before salvation you were creation only. Now God's nature is mingled with yours. You carry something of God within you. That changes how you live.
