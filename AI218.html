AI218: Why I Now Let AI Draft My Project Proposals

AI218：为什么我现在让AI帮我起草项目提案

I used to dread writing proposals — the retyping, the budget maths, the "can you also do it in Chinese?" request that arrived after I'd already finished. AI ended all three headaches at once.

我以前最怕写提案——重新打字、算预算、还有写完之后才来的“能不能也写中文版”。AI一次过解决了这三个鲜烦。

“The proposal that used to take me a whole weekend now takes an evening — and it comes out in two languages, with photos, and a budget that adds itself up correctly. That's not a small convenience. That's a different way of working.” “以前要草我整整一个周末的提案，现在一个晚上就能完成——而且是双语版，有照片，预算还会自己算对。这不是小方便，而是一种全新的工作方式。”

💡 Where This Started💡 这件事的起点

After I wrote up my 5-session "Teaching AI to Seniors" plan for Toastmasters, the club's VP Education asked for one more thing: a proper written proposal, with a budget, that she could forward to the committee. In the old days that meant a Word document, a lot of retyping, and a separate email asking a Mandarin-speaking member to translate it for the members who'd rather read Chinese.

我写好五节课的“教长者使用AI”计划后，份份的教育副主席要求我再做一件事：写一份正式的提案，带预算，好转给委员会。以前这意味着一份Word文档、大量重新打字，和另发一封邮件请中文好的会员帮忙翻译。

This time I gave everything to Claude — the plan from my last article, rough cost guesses, and a request for both languages — and had a finished document before dinner was over.

这一次我把所有资料都交给Claude——上一篇文章的计划、大致的费用估计，加上两种语言的要求——晚饱还没吃完，文件就做好了。

A proposal isn't just words — it's words, numbers, images, and often two languages, all in sync. That's exactly the kind of job AI is built for. 提案不只是文字——它是文字、数字、图片，往往还有两种语言，全部要保持一致。这正是AI最振长的坡场。

✅ Four Advantages I Actually Use✅ 我真正用上的四个优势

These aren't theoretical. Every one of these saved me real time on this one proposal alone.

这些都不是理论——佩倣这位提案，每一项都为我真正节省了时间。

  • Advantage 1优势一
    Easy to Modify. When the committee asked to shrink the pilot from 10 to 6 participants, I didn't retype anything — I just told Claude the new number, and the venue size, handout count, and budget line updated together in one pass. 修改相当容易。当委员会要求将试点人数从10人减至6人时，我不用重新打字——只需告诉Claude新的人数，场地大小、讲义数量和预算行都会一次整体更新。
  • Advantage 2优势二
    Multiple Languages, One Document. I asked for English and Chinese side by side, and the tone matched — not a stiff machine translation, but proper committee language in both. No separate translator needed. 多语言，同一文件。我请Claude同时写英文和中文，语气都得体——不是硬申的机器翻译，而是符合委员会文书语气的两个版本。无需另找翻译。
  • Advantage 3优势三
    Add Photos Easily. I dropped in a photo from Jeremy's job-ad lesson (S17) and my Mandarin AI speech, and Claude wrote the captions and placed them right next to the sections that needed proof it actually works. 插入照片很简单。我插入了教Jeremy修改招聘广告（S17）和我的普通话AI演讲的照片，Claude自动写好设明，并放在需要证明“真的有效”的童节旁边。
  • Advantage 4优势四
    Auto-Computed Lump Sum. I listed every rough cost — venue, printing, refreshments, name tags — and Claude built a proper table and added it up for me. Change one line, and the total lump sum recalculates instantly, correctly, every time. 自动算出总预算。我列出每一项大致费用——场地、印刷、茶点、名牌——Claude为我做成一张表格并算好总价。改动任何一项，总预算都会立刻，准确地重新算好。

⚠️ What I Still Check Myself⚠️ 我仍自己查核的事

AI drafts fast, but I'm the one signing my name to the proposal. I always double-check the final total against the individual line items myself, confirm real prices with the venue and printer before submitting, and read the Chinese version aloud to make sure it sounds natural, not translated.

AI草稿快，但签名的人是我。我总是自己再查一遍总预算是否等于各项。提交前向场地和印刷店确认真实价格，并朝读中文版，确保读起来自然，而不是直翻的机器文。

A Proposal Is Just a First Draft Away Now现在，提案只需一个初稿的距离

I used to put off writing proposals because they felt like a whole weekend of admin. Now the hard part is deciding what to propose — the words, the translation, the photos, and the maths all follow in one evening.

我以前总把写提案拖後，因为感觉要草整个周末。现在最难的部分只是决定要提什么——文字、翻译、照片和算术都会在一个晚上随之完成。

If I can turn a workshop idea into a bilingual, budgeted, photo-illustrated proposal in one evening at 70 — anyone reading this can too.

如果我70岁能在一个晚上把一个工作坡点子变成双语、有预算、有照片的提案——正在读这篇文章的你也能。
