📖 Background背景

TDS Technology (S) Pte Ltd (Co. Reg. No. 199603760M), together with related entity ADL Control (S) Pte Ltd, sent out the finalized audited Financial Statements for FY2025 on 17 Jul 2026. The corporate secretary will proceed with filing the Annual Return with ACRA to close out the financial year.

TDS Technology (S) Pte Ltd（公司注册号199603760M）连同关联公司ADL Control (S) Pte Ltd，于2026年7月17日发出2025财年最终经审计财务报表。公司秘书将据此向ACRA提交年度报表，结束本财年。

💡 What This Means这意味着什么

Revenue edged up slightly (+2.4%), and the business still generated healthy operating cash flow (S$894,664). But higher distribution and other operating expenses pushed the company from a S$109,884 profit in 2024 to a S$266,327 net loss in 2025. Management also repaid a large amount of bank debt and trust receipts during the year, which — combined with the loss — cut cash reserves from S$1.46M to S$0.53M.

营收小幅增长（+2.4%），经营活动仍产生健康的现金流（S$894,664）。但分销成本及其他营业开支上升，使公司由2024年盈利S$109,884转为2025年净亏损S$266,327。管理层同期也大幅偿还银行贷款及信托收据，加上亏损的影响，令现金储备由S$146万降至S$53万。
