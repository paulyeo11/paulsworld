💡 What This Means这意味着什么

Revenue edged up slightly (+2.4%), and the business still generated healthy operating cash flow (S$894,664). But higher distribution and other operating expenses pushed the company from a S$109,884 profit in 2024 to a S$266,327 net loss in 2025. Management also repaid a large amount of bank debt and trust receipts during the year, which — combined with the loss — cut cash reserves from S$1.46M to S$0.53M.

营收小幅增长（+2.4%），经营活动仍产生健康的现金流（S$894,664）。但分销成本及其他营业开支上升，使公司由2024年盈利S$109,884转为2025年净亏损S$266,327。管理层同期也大幅偿还银行贷款及信托收据，加上亏损的影响，令现金储备由S$146万降至S$53万。

🤔 Is TDS a Good Investment?TDS值得投资吗？

Based on FY2025 alone: caution, not conviction. Revenue grew a token 2.4%, but the company swung from a ~S$110K profit in 2024 to a ~S$266K net loss in 2025, while cash reserves nearly tripled down (S$1.46M → S$0.53M) even as management aggressively repaid debt. That combination — thinner margins, a shrinking cash buffer — isn't a "buy more" signal on its own.

仅从2025财年数据看：应谨慎，而非乐观。营收仅增长2.4%，但公司由2024年约S$11万的盈利转为2025年约S$27万的净亏损，现金储备更是大幅萎缩（S$146万→S$53万），即便管理层同期积极还债。利润率收窄加上现金缓冲变薄，这样的组合本身并不是"加码买入"的信号。

The bigger flag is qualitative: a fellow director-shareholder said the sector's "heyday" is over and competition is intensifying, and another shareholder (Wan) is already trying to exit. Insiders souring on the outlook usually matters more than one year of numbers.

更值得关注的是定性信号：一位董事股东表示行业的"黄金时代"已过、竞争日趋激烈，另一位股东（Wan）也已在寻求退出。内部人对前景转趋悲观，往往比一年的财务数字更值得重视。

🚪 If You're Thinking About an Exit若考虑退出

TDS is a private (unlisted) Pte Ltd — there's no brokerage, no market bid, and no way to just place a sell order like a listed stock or bond. Selling means finding a willing buyer and following the company's own share-transfer process.

TDS是一家私人（非上市）公司——没有券商、没有市场买盘，无法像上市股票或债券那样直接下卖单。卖出股份需要找到愿意接手的买家，并遵循公司自身的股权转让流程。

  1. Check the company's Constitution (Articles of Association) for share transfer restrictions — most private Singapore companies require existing shareholders to be offered the shares first (right of first refusal / pre-emption rights) before any outside buyer.查看公司章程（Articles of Association）中的股权转让限制——多数新加坡私人公司规定须先将股份提呈给现有股东（优先购买权），才能卖给外部买家。
  2. Ask the corporate secretary for the register of members and any recent share-transfer precedent — a past transaction price is the most concrete valuation reference you'll get for an unlisted company.向公司秘书索取股东名册及近期的股权转让先例——对非上市公司而言，过去的成交价是最具体的估值参考。
  3. Since Wan has already said he wants to sell, coordinate with him — a joint block of shares is often easier to place with one buyer (e.g. another shareholder, or the company itself via a buyback) than solo small parcels.既然Wan已表示想卖，可与他协调同步进行——将股份合并成一整批，通常比分别卖出小额股份更容易找到买家（如其他股东，或由公司回购）。
  4. Without a market price, a fair value is usually estimated from Net Asset Value (Total Equity ÷ total shares — using this report, S$3,967,559 ÷ shares outstanding) or a discounted earnings multiple, then adjusted down for illiquidity since there's no ready buyer.由于没有市场价格，公允价值通常以资产净值估算（总权益÷总股数——按本报告为S$3,967,559÷已发行股数），或以折现后的盈利倍数估算，并因缺乏现成买家而向下调整流动性折价。
  5. Put the request to sell in writing to the board/corporate secretary so it's on record — this also matters if it needs to be reflected before the Annual Return filing with ACRA.将出售意愿以书面形式提交给董事会/公司秘书，留下正式记录——如需在向ACRA提交年度报表前反映此事，这一点也很重要。
  6. If the process or valuation is unclear, a corporate service provider or lawyer familiar with private Singapore company share transfers can guide the paperwork — especially useful given several director changes happened this year (Jan & Apr 2026).若流程或估值不清楚，可请熟悉新加坡私人公司股权转让的企业服务机构或律师协助处理文件——鉴于今年（2026年1月及4月）董事已多次变动，这一点尤其有用。

💡 Hold vs. Exit持有 vs. 退出

Holding only makes sense if you believe the FY2025 loss is a one-off (e.g. the PPE investment of S$433K this year) rather than the start of a trend, and you're comfortable being an illiquid minority shareholder with no exit until a buyer appears. Exiting now means accepting a private-company valuation (likely at a discount) but removes the risk of the situation described by other shareholders getting worse before you can sell.

除非你相信2025财年的亏损只是一次性因素（例如今年S$43.3万的固定资产投入），而非趋势的开始，并且能接受作为流动性极低的小股东、在有买家出现前都无法退出，否则持有的理由并不充分。现在退出则意味着接受私人公司估值（可能有折让），但可避免其他股东所描述的情况在你脱手前进一步恶化的风险。
