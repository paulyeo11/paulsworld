Google Drive Connector —
Your Travel Photos, Always Ready

Google Drive 连接器 —
旅行照片，随时可用

✍️ Paul Yeo 📅 June 2026 ⏱️ 7 min read

When I started planning my one-month Japan trip (S24), I realised I had a problem: my photos live on my phone and Google Drive, but my travel stories live on GitHub. Every time I wrote a story, I had to manually upload photos — one by one. Then I discovered the Google Drive Connector inside Claude Code on the Web. It changed my entire workflow forever.

当我开始规划我的日本一个月旅程（S24）时，我意识到一个问题：我的照片在手机和 Google Drive 上，但我的旅行故事在 GitHub 上。每次写故事，我都要一张张手动上传照片。后来我发现了 Claude Code 网页版中的 Google Drive 连接器，它彻底改变了我的整个工作流程。

The Problem I Was Facing 我面临的问题

When I travel, I take hundreds of photos. My iPhone automatically backs them up to Google Photos and Google Drive. That part works beautifully. The problem starts when I want to write a travel story for my website.

每次旅行，我都会拍数百张照片。我的 iPhone 会自动将它们备份到 Google Photos 和 Google Drive。这部分运作得很好。问题出在我想为网站写旅行故事时。

Previously, the process was: find the right photos on my phone → download them to my computer → resize them → upload to GitHub → then tell Claude which photos to use in the story. It was slow, repetitive, and easy to make mistakes.

以前的流程是：在手机上找到合适的照片 → 下载到电脑 → 调整大小 → 上传到 GitHub → 再告诉 Claude 在故事中使用哪些照片。这个过程缓慢、重复，而且容易出错。

For a short trip, this was manageable. But for my upcoming one-month Japan trip — potentially 500 or more photos — I needed a better way.

对于短途旅行，这还勉强可以应付。但对于即将到来的日本一个月旅程——可能有 500 张或更多照片——我需要一个更好的方式。

Instead of me moving photos to Claude, what if Claude could come directly to where my photos already live?

与其我把照片移给 Claude，为什么不让 Claude 直接去我照片所在的地方呢？

What Is the Google Drive Connector? 什么是 Google Drive 连接器？

The Google Drive Connector is a feature inside Claude Code on the Web that gives Claude permission to read your Google Drive files — just like it reads your GitHub files. Once connected, Claude can browse your folders, read documents, and view photos you have stored there.

Google Drive 连接器是 Claude Code 网页版中的一项功能，它授权 Claude 读取您的 Google Drive 文件——就像读取您的 GitHub 文件一样。连接后，Claude 可以浏览您的文件夹、阅读文档，并查看您存储的照片。

In technical language, it is called an MCP (Model Context Protocol) server. But you do not need to understand that. Think of it simply as a bridge — connecting Google Drive on one side to Claude on the other, so information can flow between them.

从技术角度来说，它被称为 MCP（模型上下文协议）服务器。但您不需要了解这些。把它简单地理解为一座桥梁——一端连接 Google Drive，另一端连接 Claude，让信息在两者之间流通。

📂 What Claude Can Do with Google Drive Access
  • List all files and folders in your Google Drive
  • 列出 Google Drive 中的所有文件和文件夹
  • Search for specific photos by name or folder
  • 按名称或文件夹搜索特定照片
  • Read the content of documents (Word, Google Docs, PDFs)
  • 读取文档内容（Word、Google Docs、PDF）
  • View photos you upload to a specific folder
  • 查看您上传到特定文件夹的照片
  • Use photos directly in your travel stories — no manual upload to GitHub
  • 直接在旅行故事中使用照片——无需手动上传到 GitHub

How to Set It Up — Step by Step 如何一步步设置

Setting up the Google Drive Connector takes about five minutes. Here is exactly what to do:

设置 Google Drive 连接器大约需要五分钟。以下是具体步骤：

Open Claude Code on the Web 打开网页版 Claude Code

Go to code.claude.ai in your browser and sign in with your Anthropic account. Make sure you are in an active session with your paulsworld GitHub repository connected.

在浏览器中访问 code.claude.ai 并用您的 Anthropic 账户登录。确保您已连接 paulsworld GitHub 仓库并处于活跃会话中。

Find the Integrations / Connectors Menu 找到集成 / 连接器菜单

Look for the plug icon (🔌) or "Integrations" button in the Claude Code interface — usually in the left sidebar or the settings panel. Click it to open the list of available connectors.

在 Claude Code 界面中找到插头图标（🔌）或"集成"按钮——通常在左侧边栏或设置面板中。点击它打开可用连接器列表。

Click "Add" next to Google Drive 点击 Google Drive 旁边的"添加"

Scroll through the connector list until you see Google Drive. Click the Add or Connect button next to it. A Google sign-in window will appear.

滚动连接器列表，找到 Google Drive。点击旁边的添加或连接按钮。将弹出 Google 登录窗口。

Sign in with Your Google Account 用您的 Google 账户登录

Sign in using the Google account where your travel photos are stored (the same account your iPhone backs up to). Google will show you a permission screen listing exactly what Claude is allowed to access.

使用存储旅行照片的 Google 账户登录（即 iPhone 备份所用的账户）。Google 将显示权限屏幕，列出 Claude 被允许访问的内容。

Grant Permission and Confirm 授予权限并确认

Click Allow on the Google permission screen. Claude Code will confirm the connection is successful. You will see a green checkmark or "Connected" status next to Google Drive in the connectors list.

在 Google 权限屏幕上点击允许。Claude Code 将确认连接成功。您将在连接器列表中的 Google Drive 旁边看到绿色勾号或"已连接"状态。

Organise Your Travel Photos on Drive 在 Drive 上整理您的旅行照片

For best results, create a dedicated folder for each trip. For example: "S24 Japan 2026". Inside it, make sub-folders by city or day: Tokyo / Kyoto / Osaka / Day 01 / Day 02, etc. This makes it easy to tell Claude exactly which photos to use.

为了获得最佳效果，为每次旅行创建专用文件夹。例如："S24 Japan 2026"。在其中，按城市或日期创建子文件夹：东京 / 京都 / 大阪 / 第1天 / 第2天等。这样可以轻松告诉 Claude 使用哪些照片。

💡 Pro Tip — Folder Naming

Name your folders with the story prefix so Claude can find them instantly. Example: S24_Japan_Tokyo, S24_Japan_Kyoto. When you say "use photos from S24 Japan Tokyo folder", Claude knows exactly where to look.

用故事前缀命名文件夹，这样 Claude 可以立即找到它们。例如：S24_Japan_Tokyo、S24_Japan_Kyoto。当您说"使用 S24 Japan Tokyo 文件夹中的照片"时，Claude 确切知道去哪里找。

Important Things to Know 重要注意事项

A few practical points before you start using the Google Drive Connector:

在开始使用 Google Drive 连接器之前，有几点实际注意事项：

⚠️ Things to Keep in Mind
  • Privacy: Only grant Google access to the specific account and folders you are comfortable sharing. Claude only reads what you point it to.
  • 隐私： 仅授予您放心分享的特定账户和文件夹的 Google 访问权限。Claude 只读取您指向的内容。
  • Photos vs. Google Photos: The Drive connector reads files stored in Google Drive. If your photos are only in Google Photos, you need to move or copy them to a Drive folder first.
  • 照片 vs. Google 相册： Drive 连接器读取存储在 Google Drive 中的文件。如果您的照片只在 Google 相册中，您需要先将它们移动或复制到 Drive 文件夹中。
  • File names matter: Name your photos descriptively — kyoto-golden-pavilion-morning.jpg helps Claude understand what each photo shows without opening every one.
  • 文件名很重要： 给照片取有描述性的名字——kyoto-golden-pavilion-morning.jpg 帮助 Claude 了解每张照片的内容，无需逐一打开。
  • Re-authorize if needed: If Claude says it cannot access Drive, click the connector settings and re-authorize — Google tokens sometimes expire.
  • 如需重新授权： 如果 Claude 说无法访问 Drive，点击连接器设置并重新授权——Google 令牌有时会过期。
  • Large files: Very large RAW camera files may be slow. Stick to JPEGs — your iPhone photos are already the right format.
  • 大文件： 非常大的 RAW 相机文件可能会很慢。坚持使用 JPEG——您的 iPhone 照片已经是正确格式。

My Verdict — A Game Changer for Travel Writers 我的评价——旅行写作者的游戏规则改变者

Before the Google Drive Connector, writing a travel story felt like two separate jobs: first the technical work of managing photos, then the creative work of writing. The first job was boring and error-prone. It drained my energy before I even started writing.

在使用 Google Drive 连接器之前，写旅行故事感觉像是两份不同的工作：首先是管理照片的技术性工作，然后是写作的创意工作。第一份工作枯燥且容易出错。它在我开始写作之前就耗尽了我的精力。

Now, the technical friction is gone. My phone takes the photos. Google Drive holds them. Claude reads them. I just describe what I want to say about my trip — and the story appears. That is the way it should be.

现在，技术摩擦消失了。手机拍照。Google Drive 存储。Claude 读取。我只需描述我想对旅行说什么——故事就出现了。这才是应该有的方式。

For my Japan trip (S24), I plan to write a chapter for each major city — Tokyo, Kyoto, Osaka, Hiroshima, Nara, and a few surprises. With the Google Drive Connector, I can write each chapter from anywhere in Japan, while the memories are still fresh, using just my phone. That feels like real freedom.

对于我的日本旅行（S24），我计划为每个主要城市写一章——东京、京都、大阪、广岛、奈良，以及一些惊喜。有了 Google Drive 连接器，我可以在日本的任何地方写每一章——趁记忆还新鲜，只用手机就能完成。这感觉像是真正的自由。

The best tools disappear. You stop noticing the technology and start noticing only the story you are telling.
