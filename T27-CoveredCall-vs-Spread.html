🔑 The One Lesson: Liquidity Beats Everything🔑 最重要的一课：流动性胜过一切

The original idea was to sell 1 FDS 310 Call for a "$5.10 credit." It looked fine — but the option was untradeable. The bid/ask was $0.10 vs $5.10 — a $5 gap — and only 44 contracts traded all day. That $5 spread is a loss you take before the trade even moves. The exact same strategy on SPY, where the spread is 4 cents and millions trade daily, actually works. The strike didn't sink the FDS trade — the illiquidity did.最初的想法是卖出1张FDS 310认购，收取"5.10美元权利金"。看起来没问题——但这张期权根本无法成交。买价/卖价是0.10对5.10——相差5美元——而且全天只成交了44张。这5美元的价差是你在价格还没变动之前就已承受的亏损。同样的策略放到SPY上——价差只有4美分、每天成交数百万张——就真的可行。让FDS这笔交易失败的不是行使价，而是流动性太差。

Setup 1 · Covered Call (SPY)方案一 · 备兑认购（SPY）

You own the stock and sell a call against it for income. Because you hold the shares, the call is "covered" — the scary unlimited-loss risk of a naked call is gone. SPY = $753.48, expiry Aug 28 (44 days).你持有股票，并卖出认购来赚取收入。因为你手上有股票，这张认购是"备兑"的——裸认购那种可怕的无限亏损风险就消失了。SPY = 753.48美元，到期日8月28日（44天）。

Buy 100 shares SPY买入100股SPY
≈ −$75,350
Sell the 755 Call卖出755认购
≈ +$1,570
At expiry到期时 Result结果
SPY ≥ $755 Shares called away. Profit = ($755−$753.48)×100 + $1,570 = ~$1,722 (~2.3% in 44 days)股票被行权卖出。利润 = ($755−$753.48)×100 + $1,570 = 约$1,722（44天约2.3%）
SPY < $755 Keep the shares + the $1,570. New break-even drops to ~$737.80 (~2% cushion).保留股票 + 收到的$1,570。新的保本点降到约$737.80（约2%缓冲）。

Catch: ties up ~$75k of capital — SPY is a pricey ticket at 100 shares.缺点：占用约7.5万美元资金——100股SPY是一张很贵的"门票"。

Setup 2 · Bear Call Spread (SPY)方案二 · 熊市认购价差（SPY）

The same "SPY won't rally" bet, but the loss is capped and you don't need to own the stock. You sell one call and buy a higher one as insurance.同样是"SPY不会大涨"的押注，但亏损被封顶，而且不需要持有股票。你卖出一张认购，同时买入一张更高行使价的认购作为保险。

Risk $125 to make $175 — a healthy ratio, with only ~$125 at risk. This is what a sane premium-selling trade looks like, and it's exactly what FDS's junk spread could never deliver.用$125博取$175——比例健康，且只有约$125面临风险。这就是一笔理性的卖权交易该有的样子——也正是FDS那种糟糕价差永远给不了的。

✅ Takeaways✅ 总结要点

  • Check liquidity first.先看流动性。 Tight bid/ask (pennies) + high volume before you even look at the strike.在看行使价之前，先确认买卖价差窄（几美分）且成交量高。
  • Never sell a naked call.绝不卖裸认购。 Unlimited upside risk for a small, uncertain premium is a bad trade.用无限的上行风险去换一点点不确定的权利金，是糟糕的交易。
  • Covered call备兑认购 = own the stock first; income + cushion, no unlimited risk, but big capital.= 先持有股票；有收入+缓冲，无无限风险，但占用资金大。
  • Call spread认购价差 = capped loss, tiny capital; the efficient way to sell premium.= 亏损封顶、占用资金小；卖权利金最有效率的方式。
  • Trade liquid names交易流动性好的标的 — SPY, QQQ, AAPL, NVDA — so the math works in your favour, not the market-maker's.——SPY、QQQ、AAPL、NVDA——让数学站在你这边，而不是做市商那边。
