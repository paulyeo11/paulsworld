1
Jiuzhaigou National Park九寨沟国家公园
Day 2 · North Sichuan第2天 · 川北
▼
Rainbow Lakes & Waterfalls彩虹湖泊与瀑布
UNESCO World Heritage Site with 108 multicoloured turquoise, emerald and sapphire lakes set among snow peaks. Three valleys: Shuzheng, Rize and Zechawa. Best colour in autumn (Sep–Nov).联合国教科文组织世界遗产，108个五彩斑斓的湖泊镶嵌于雪山之间。三条沟：树正沟、日则沟、则查洼沟。秋季（9-11月）色彩最美。
Book tickets online weeks in advance. Enter early (8 am) to avoid crowds. Take the scenic bus up, walk down.提前数周网上订票。早上8点入场避开人潮。乘观光车上山，步行下山。
⭐ #1 Must Do
2
Huanglong Scenic Area黄龙风景区
Day 3 · North Sichuan第3天 · 川北
▼
Golden Travertine Terraces黄金钙化彩池
3,400–3,600 m altitude. Over 3,400 golden travertine pools cascade down a 3.6 km valley. Also a UNESCO site. The hike takes 3–4 hours. Altitude sickness common — take it slow.海拔3400-3600米。超过3400个金色钙化池沿3.6公里谷地层叠流淌。同为世界遗产。徒步需3-4小时。高原反应常见，慢行为宜。
Cable car up, walk down. Altitude acclimatisation pills (Rhodiola) help. Morning light best for photos.乘缆车上山，步行下山。红景天有助于高原适应。晨光拍照最佳。
⭐ UNESCO Heritage
3
Huahu Wetland Lake花湖湿地
Day 4 · Zoige, North Sichuan第4天 · 若尔盖 川北
▼
Mirror Lake on the Plateau高原镜面湖
A stunning highland lake (3,468 m) surrounded by endless grasslands. In summer it fills with water lilies and black-necked cranes. The surrounding Zoige Wetland is China's largest highland peat marsh.令人窒息的高原湖泊（海拔3468米），四周是无边草甸。夏季盛开睡莲，黑颈鹤栖息于此。周边若尔盖湿地是中国最大高原泥炭沼泽。
Best in July when wildflowers bloom. Black-necked cranes migrate here Oct–Mar. Free entry.7月野花盛开最美。黑颈鹤10月至次年3月迁徙至此。免费入场。
🦢 Bird Paradise
4
Yellow River First Bend (Tangke)黄河第一湾（唐克）
Day 4 · Zoige, North Sichuan第4天 · 若尔盖 川北
▼
The Yellow River's Grand Meander黄河大湾
At Tangke village, the Yellow River makes its first dramatic bend, creating a vast S-shaped meander across golden grasslands. Climb the viewing platform at sunset for an unforgettable panorama. Nomadic Tibetan tent camps dot the banks.在唐克村，黄河形成第一个壮观大湾，在金色草原上蜿蜒成S形。日落时登上观景台，俯瞰令人难忘的全景。黄河两岸点缀着游牧藏族帐篷营地。
Go at golden hour (6–7 pm in summer). You can ride horses to the riverbank with local Tibetan herders.夏季黄金时段（下午6-7点）前往。可随当地藏族牧民骑马至河边。
🌅 Best at Sunset
5
Langmusi (Taktsang Lhamo)郎木寺（达仓郎木）
Day 5 · Gannan / Sichuan border第5天 · 甘南 / 川甘边境
▼
Little Switzerland of Tibet藏区的小瑞士
A magical village straddling the Sichuan-Gansu border, split by a tiny stream. Two Tibetan monasteries face each other across the gorge. Dramatic red cliffs, sky burial sites, and crystal-clear gorge walks. Very few tourists.一个神奇的村庄横跨川甘边界，被一条小溪分开。两座藏传佛教寺院隔峡相望。壮观的红色悬崖、天葬台和清澈的峡谷栈道。游客极少。
Walk the gorge trail behind the Sichuan monastery (1–2 hrs). Early morning mist is magical. Respect sky burial sites.走四川寺庙后方的峡谷栈道（1-2小时）。清晨雾气弥漫，如梦如幻。请尊重天葬台。
🏔️ Hidden Gem
6
Labrang Monastery, Xiahe夏河拉卜楞寺
Day 7 · Gannan第7天 · 甘南
▼
World's Largest Tibetan Buddhist Monastic Complex世界最大藏传佛教寺院建筑群
Founded in 1709, Labrang houses over 1,000 monks across six colleges. Its 3 km prayer wheel kora (circuit) is one of the most sacred walks in Tibetan Buddhism. Morning debates and prayer ceremonies draw pilgrims from across the Tibetan plateau.创建于1709年，拉卜楞寺拥有六大学院，逾千名僧侣。3公里转经廊是藏传佛教最神圣的转经道之一。晨间辩经和祈福法会吸引藏区各地朝圣者。
Join the morning kora walk at 7 am with pilgrims. Hire a monk guide for monastery interior. Wear modest clothing.早上7点随朝圣者参加晨间转经。雇佣僧侣向导参观寺院内部。着装需端庄。
⭐ #1 in Gannan
7
Sangke Grasslands桑科草原
Day 7 · Xiahe, Gannan第7天 · 夏河 甘南
▼
Nomadic Yak Country游牧牦牛大草原
Just 14 km from Xiahe, Sangke is a sweeping highland meadow at 3,000 m where Tibetan nomads graze yaks. Wildflowers carpet the ground in summer. You can stay in a traditional yak-wool tent (black tent camp) for the night.距夏河仅14公里，桑科是海拔3000米的宽阔高原草甸，藏族游牧民在此放牧牦牛。夏季野花遍地。可在传统黑色牦牛毛帐篷营地住一夜。
Stay in a nomad tent camp for a genuine experience. Yak butter tea and tsampa breakfast included.住在游牧帐篷营地，体验真实生活。含酥油茶和糌粑早餐。
🏕️ Nomad Stay
8
Maqu Yellow River Grand Bend玛曲黄河大拐弯
Day 8 · Gannan第8天 · 甘南
▼
Yellow River's 433 km Detour黄河433公里大迂回
At Maqu (Machu), the Yellow River enters Gannan and makes a massive 433 km horseshoe bend before exiting back into Qinghai. The plateau meadows here are some of the most pristine on earth. Tibetan herders graze yaks right along the riverbanks.在玛曲，黄河进入甘南，划出一个433公里的巨大马蹄形大弯后重新流入青海。这里的高原草甸是地球上最原始的草地之一。藏族牧民在黄河岸边放牧牦牛。
Drive to Awancang Wetland (阿万仓) 30 km from Maqu for the best river views. Very few tourists.从玛曲驱车30公里至阿万仓湿地，欣赏最佳黄河景观。游客极少。
🌊 Off the Beaten Path
9
Hongyuan Red Grasslands红原大草原
Day 9 · North Sichuan第9天 · 川北
▼
Long March Wetlands长征湿地
Hongyuan (Red Prairie) was where Mao's Red Army crossed the deadly Zoige marshes in 1935 during the Long March. The grasslands turn brilliant red-gold in autumn. The Red Army Memorial Museum tells the story. Vast and wild — you can camp here.1935年长征期间，毛泽东的红军穿越了死亡沼泽若尔盖。秋季草原变为绚烂的红金色。红军纪念馆述说着那段历史。广阔荒野，可在此野营。
Visit the Long March Memorial site. September when grass turns red-gold is the best photo season.参观长征纪念地。9月草色变为红金色，是拍照最佳季节。
🏴 Historical Site
10
Songpan Ancient Town松潘古城
Day 10 · North Sichuan第10天 · 川北
▼
Ming Dynasty Frontier Town明朝边疆古城
A remarkably preserved Ming-era walled town at the crossroads of Tibetan, Han and Qiang cultures. Ancient city walls, mosques, temples and teahouses coexist. Famous for horse trekking into surrounding mountain valleys.一座保存完好的明代古城，是藏、汉、羌文化的交汇之地。古城墙、清真寺、寺庙与茶馆共存。以骑马进入周边山谷而著名。
Walk the full city wall circuit (45 min). Try the local Hui Muslim beef noodles. Horse treks bookable at town gates.绕城墙走一圈（45分钟）。品尝当地回族牛肉面。城门口可预订骑马游。
🏯 Cultural Gem
Tibetan plateau cuisine — hearty, warming, and unlike anything else.藏族高原美食 — 丰盛暖胃，独一无二。
🧈
Yak Butter Tea (酥油茶)酥油茶
Everywhere on the plateau高原各地均有
Churned tea mixed with yak butter and salt. Warming and energising at altitude. It's an acquired taste — salty, rich, almost soup-like. Tibetan hosts offer it as welcome; always accept the first cup.酥油与盐茶搅打而成。在高海拔地区暖身提神。口味独特——咸香浓郁，近似浓汤。藏族主人以此待客，务必接受第一杯。
Must Try
🌾
Tsampa (糌粑)糌粑
Tibetan guesthouses and tent camps藏族客栈和帐篷营地
Roasted highland barley flour kneaded with yak butter tea. The Tibetan staple — dense, nutty and incredibly filling. Pilgrims carry it in pouches for the kora walk. Mix it yourself at the table — a social ritual.青稞炒面与酥油茶揉和而成。藏族主食，质地紧实，口感香浓，极为饱腹。朝圣者将其装袋，转经途中食用。在餐桌上亲自揉制——是一种社交仪式。
Staple Food
🥩
Yak Meat Dishes (牦牛肉)牦牛肉菜肴
Restaurants in Zoige, Langmusi, Xiahe若尔盖、郎木寺、夏河餐厅
Yak roams at 4,000 m — its meat is lean, gamey and mineral-rich. Try yak hot pot, yak jerky, yak steak and yak dumpling (momo). Also try yak yoghurt — thick, tangy and delicious.牦牛在海拔4000米处自由放牧，其肉质精瘦，带野味，矿物质丰富。可尝牦牛火锅、牦牛肉干、牦牛排和牦牛饺子（馍馍）。还可试试牦牛酸奶——浓稠微酸，美味可口。
🍜
Niuroumian — Lanzhou Beef Noodles (牛肉拉面)牛肉拉面（兰州风味）
Hezuo, Xiahe, Langmusi合作、夏河、郎木寺
Gannan is close to Lanzhou, and hand-pulled beef noodles are everywhere. Clear broth, springy noodles, tender beef slices, chilli oil on top. Breakfast and lunch staple across Gannan.甘南毗邻兰州，手工拉面随处可见。清汤、劲道面条、嫩滑牛肉片，加一勺辣椒油。是甘南早午餐主食。
Under ¥15
🥟
Momo — Tibetan Dumplings (藏式饺子)藏式馍馍饺子
Langmusi, Xiahe, Zoige郎木寺、夏河、若尔盖
Steamed or fried dumplings filled with yak meat, vegetables or cheese. Popular in Tibetan cafés. The Nepali-Tibetan version with sauce is particularly good in Langmusi's backpacker cafés.蒸制或油煎饺子，馅料为牦牛肉、蔬菜或奶酪。在藏式咖啡馆颇受欢迎。郎木寺背包客咖啡馆的尼泊尔藏式馍馍配酱汁尤为美味。
🍺
Highland Barley Wine (青稞酒)青稞酒
Tibetan guesthouses and festivals藏族客栈和节庆活动
Fermented highland barley — slightly fizzy, mildly alcoholic and malty. Served in wooden bowls. Tibetan hosts fill your bowl as a sign of hospitality. Note: altitude amplifies alcohol effects — drink very little.青稞发酵而成，略带气泡，酒精度低，麦香浓郁。盛于木碗中奉客。藏族主人不断添酒以示盛情。注意：高原环境放大酒精效果，饮用需格外节制。
🍲
Sichuan Hot Pot & Mapo Tofu四川火锅与麻婆豆腐
Jiuzhaigou town, Songpan, return to Chengdu九寨沟、松潘及返回成都途中
Once you descend back to Sichuan towns, celebrate with classic Sichuan food. The numbing-spicy mala hot pot of Chengdu is the perfect end to a highland journey.下山回到四川城镇后，用正宗川菜犒劳自己。成都麻辣火锅是高原之旅的完美收尾。
End-of-trip Reward
🏔️ Altitude Sickness — Critical高原反应 — 非常重要 ▼
  • Acclimatise in Chengdu for 1 night before flying to Jiuzhai (3,448 m airport). Altitude rises fast.在成都适应1晚再飞九寨（机场3448米）。海拔骤升。
  • Take Rhodiola rosea (红景天) capsules 3 days before arriving at altitude. Available at all Chinese pharmacies.抵达高原前3天服用红景天胶囊。各中药房均有售。
  • Huanglong is highest at 3,600 m — walk slowly, rest often. Cable car available.黄龙最高海拔3600米 — 缓慢行走，多休息。有缆车可乘。
  • Symptoms: headache, nausea, breathlessness. Rest and drink water. Descent is the only cure.症状：头痛、恶心、气短。休息并多喝水。下山是唯一根治方法。
  • Avoid alcohol, heavy food and exercise on first 2 days at altitude.在高原的前两天避免饮酒、进食过量和剧烈运动。
📅 Best Time to Visit最佳游览时间 ▼
  • May–June: Green grasslands, wildflowers, fewer tourists. Some passes may still have snow.5-6月：草原碧绿，野花盛开，游客较少。部分山口可能仍有积雪。
  • September–October: Best for Jiuzhaigou autumn colours (red, orange, gold). Peak season — book months ahead.9-10月：九寨沟秋色最佳（红、橙、金）。旺季——提前数月订票。
  • July–August: Rainy season. Jiuzhaigou may be partially closed due to landslides. Roads can be dangerous.7-8月：雨季。九寨沟可能因滑坡局部关闭。道路可能危险。
  • November–April: Cold, some roads blocked by snow. Jiuzhaigou is quietest and most beautiful in light snow.11月-次年4月：寒冷，部分道路被雪封堵。九寨沟在薄雪中最为宁静美丽。
🚗 Getting Around交通出行 ▼
  • Fly Chengdu → Jiuzhai Huanglong Airport (GHN, 45 min). Book early — sells out fast. Or drive from Chengdu (~6 hrs via Dujiangyan).飞成都→九寨黄龙机场（GHN，45分钟）。提早预订——票源紧张。或从成都自驾（经都江堰约6小时）。
  • Hire a driver (包车, bāo chē) for the Zoige–Langmusi–Xiahe leg. About ¥800–1,200/day for a private car. Driver shares accommodation cost.包车走若尔盖-郎木寺-夏河段。私家车约¥800-1200/天。司机费用含住宿。
  • Shared minibuses run between main towns but schedules are irregular. Good for Zoige ↔ Langmusi (¥30–50).各主要城镇间有共享面包车，但班次不固定。适合若尔盖↔郎木寺段（¥30-50）。
  • Roads are mostly paved now but mountain sections can be narrow and winding. Allow extra time.道路大多已铺设硬化，但山区路段仍可能狭窄弯曲，需预留额外时间。
🎫 Entry Tickets & Permits门票与许可证 ▼
  • Jiuzhaigou: ¥169 (low season) to ¥202 (peak). Book on official WeChat mini-program — no walk-ins during peak.九寨沟：淡季¥169，旺季¥202。在官方微信小程序订票——旺季不接受现场购票。
  • Huanglong: ¥200 (Apr–Nov) or ¥115 (winter). Cable car extra ¥80 up/down.黄龙：4-11月¥200，冬季¥115。缆车另收¥80（单程）。
  • No special Tibet permit required for Gannan or North Sichuan — standard Chinese visa is sufficient.甘南和川北无需西藏特别许可证——标准中国签证即可。
  • Foreigners need to register at hotels (automatic for most, but check in rural areas).外籍人士须在酒店登记（多数自动处理，但在农村地区需确认）。
📱 Connectivity & Money网络与货币 ▼
  • WeChat Pay & Alipay are essential — most rural vendors don't accept cards. Link to a Chinese bank account or set up the international version.微信支付和支付宝必不可少——大多数农村商户不接受银行卡。关联中国银行账户或开通国际版。
  • Mobile signal is patchy between towns. China Telecom has best coverage in rural Gannan. Download offline maps (Maps.me or Baidu Maps).城镇之间手机信号断断续续。中国电信在甘南农村覆盖最好。下载离线地图（Maps.me或百度地图）。
  • ATMs in larger towns (Zoige, Hezuo, Xiahe) but not in villages. Carry ¥3,000–5,000 cash.较大城镇（若尔盖、合作、夏河）有ATM，但村庄没有。随身携带¥3000-5000现金。
  • VPN is needed for Google, WhatsApp etc. Set it up before entering China.访问谷歌、WhatsApp等需使用VPN。入境前提前安装配置好。
🙏 Cultural Respect文化礼仪 ▼
  • Always walk clockwise around monasteries, stupas and mani stone piles.绕寺庙、佛塔和玛尼石堆时，务必按顺时针方向行走。
  • Remove shoes before entering monastery halls. Ask permission before photographing monks or ceremonies.进入寺庙大殿前脱鞋。拍摄僧侣或法会前须征得许可。
  • Don't touch religious statues or point feet at sacred objects. Don't step over prayer flags.勿触摸宗教雕像，勿将脚指向圣物。勿踩踏经幡。
  • Dress modestly at monasteries — cover shoulders and knees. Carry a scarf.在寺庙着装端庄——遮盖肩膀和膝盖。随身携带围巾。
  • Never approach a sky burial site (天葬台) without a local guide's explicit permission.未经当地向导明确许可，切勿靠近天葬台。
🎒 What to Pack行李清单 ▼
  • Layering is key: +30°C midday to +5°C at night in summer at altitude. Down jacket essential.分层穿着是关键：高原夏季白天可达30°C，夜晚降至5°C。羽绒服必备。
  • High-SPF sunscreen (SPF 50+) and quality sunglasses — UV is intense at 3,500 m.高SPF防晒霜（SPF 50+）和优质太阳镜 —— 海拔3500米紫外线极强。
  • Diamox (acetazolamide) tablets if altitude sickness is a concern. Consult doctor before trip.若担心高原反应可带乙酰唑胺（Diamox）。出发前咨询医生。
  • Rain jacket — weather changes fast. Waterproof hiking shoes strongly recommended.雨衣必备，天气变化迅速。强烈建议穿防水徒步鞋。
  • Power bank (cold weather drains batteries fast). Universal adapter (China type A/I).充电宝（低温会快速耗尽电量）。万能转换插头（中国A/I型）。
