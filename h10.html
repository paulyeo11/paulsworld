How Catch-Up Works追赶机制说明

The 30-day total goal is 360,000 steps (15,000 × 24) — a built-in buffer of about 6 lighter days. As long as the running total stays on pace for 12,000/day, you're fine even if some days fall short of 15,000. Fall behind that pace and this page will flag it with exactly how many extra steps/day you need for the rest of the challenge. 30天的总目标是360,000步（15,000 × 24）— 内建约6天较轻松日子的缓冲。只要总步数保持在每日12,000步的进度上，即使某几天没达到15,000步也没关系。一旦落后于这个进度，本页会明确提示你在剩余天数中每天还需要多走多少步。
