S32: My Retirement — How I Spend My Day? S32: 我的退休生活——我如何度过每一天？

A retired man's Monday errand — a broken fan, a bus ride to Ubi, and a showroom full of surprises. 一个退休人士的周一出行——一台坏掉的风扇、一趟去乌美的巴士，以及一个满是惊喜的展示厅。

29 June 2026 · Ubi, Singapore2026年6月29日 · 新加坡乌美

✅ Link copied!链接已复制！

It started with a fan that stopped working properly. Nothing dramatic — it just wasn't spinning right. In my working days, I might have left it for weeks, too busy to deal with it. But now that I'm retired, I have something I never had before: time. So this Monday morning, I decided to sort it out.

The fan is a Mistral — bought from Mayer. I knew there was a service centre somewhere in the east side of Singapore, and a quick check on Notion confirmed it: 71 Ubi Crescent, Excalibur Centre, #06-01. Near Ubi MRT on the Circle Line. Simple enough.

I took the bus out. It was one of those clear Singapore mornings — blue sky, white clouds, tree-lined roads. Sitting on the upper deck, I looked out at the passing streets and thought: this is what retirement feels like. No rush. No meeting to get to. Just a man on a bus, going to fix his fan.

这一切始于一台出了毛病的风扇。没有什么戏剧性的故事——它只是转得不对劲了。在工作的年代，我可能会把它搁置好几个星期，忙得没空理会。但现在退休了，我拥有了以前从未有过的东西：时间。所以这个周一早上，我决定去把它解决掉。

这台风扇是Mistral牌的，从Mayer购买。我知道新加坡东边有一个服务中心，在Notion上一查便找到了：乌美新月71号，Excalibur Centre #06-01，就在环线乌美地铁站附近。很简单。

我坐巴士出发了。那是一个阳光明媚的新加坡早晨——蓝天白云，道路两旁绿树成荫。坐在上层，我望着窗外掠过的街道，心想：这就是退休的感觉。不慌不忙，没有会议要赶。只是一个坐着巴士去修风扇的人。

"This is what retirement feels like. No rush. No meeting to get to. Just a man on a bus, going to fix his fan." "这就是退休的感觉。不慌不忙，没有会议要赶。只是一个坐着巴士去修风扇的人。"

I changed buses along the way and eventually found myself outside Excalibur Centre — a clean, modern industrial building off Ubi Crescent. I went up to #06-01 and was greeted by a proper Mistral showroom. I wasn't expecting that. I thought it would be a small counter with a queue. Instead, it was a bright, well-organised space filled with every kind of fan you can imagine — ceiling fans with lights, bladeless fans, compact desk fans, retro-style stand fans. Even a "Greenleaf" eco range.

The staff were friendly. I handed over my fan, explained the problem, and they checked it over. The verdict: $50 for the repair. No warranty — the fan was bought more than a year ago. Fair enough. I left it with them and made my way back.

On the way home, I took Bus 63. And I realised — this is one of those small but satisfying days. I woke up with a broken fan. By noon, it was in good hands. The errand was done. Life, at its simplest, handled.

中途我换了一趟巴士，最终来到了Excalibur Centre门前——这是乌美新月路旁一栋整洁现代的工业楼。我上到06-01，迎接我的是一个正式的Mistral展示厅。我没料到会是这样。我以为会是一个小柜台加一条队伍。没想到，这里是一个明亮、整洁的空间，各式各样的风扇琳琅满目——带灯的吊扇、无叶风扇、小巧的桌面风扇、复古立式风扇，甚至还有一个"Greenleaf"环保系列。

工作人员很友善。我把风扇交给他们，说明了问题，他们检查了一遍。结论：维修费50元。不在保修范围内——这台风扇买了超过一年了。也合情合理。我把风扇留下，然后踏上回家的路。

回家我坐的是63路巴士。我突然意识到——这是那种虽小却令人满足的一天。早上醒来风扇坏了，中午之前它已经交到了能干的人手里。事情办妥了。生活，以它最简单的方式，被处理好了。

The view from the bus window on the way to Ubi. A clear Monday morning — tree-lined roads, blue sky, light traffic. I remember thinking: no one in this bus is rushing like they used to. Or maybe they are, and I'm just the one who isn't anymore. 前往乌美途中，从巴士车窗望出去的风景。清晨的周一——绿树成荫的道路、蓝天、稀疏的车流。我记得当时心里想：这辆巴士上没有人像以前那样匆忙赶路。或许他们还是一样赶，只是我不再是那个赶路的人了。

Photo 2 of 5 — Excalibur Centre, Ubi第2张，共5张——乌美Excalibur Centre

This is the building — Excalibur Centre at 71 Ubi Crescent. Clean, modern, easy to find once you know where you're going. The Mistral service centre is on the 6th floor. I took this photo outside so I'd remember the place for when I come back to collect. 这就是那栋楼——乌美新月71号的Excalibur Centre。整洁、现代，一旦知道去哪里就很容易找到。Mistral服务中心在6楼。我在外面拍了这张照片，这样回来取风扇时就知道是哪里了。

Photo 3 of 5 — The Mistral Showroom第3张，共5张——Mistral展示厅

I was not expecting this. A proper showroom — artificial grass floor, ceiling fans spinning overhead, every model on display. It felt more like a lifestyle boutique than a repair centre. The staff were patient and professional. They took my fan without fuss and gave me a clear answer on the cost. Sometimes the simplest errands turn into small discoveries. 我完全没料到会是这样。一个正式的展示厅——人造草皮地板，头顶上吊扇悠悠转动，每款型号都有展示。感觉更像是一家生活精品店，而不是维修中心。工作人员耐心、专业，二话不说就收下了我的风扇，并清楚地告知了费用。有时候，最简单的出行，反而会带来意外的小发现。

Photo 4 of 5 — Paya Lebar MRT (CC9)第4张，共5张——巴耶利峇地铁站（CC9）

On the way to Old Airport Road Food Centre, I changed to the Circle Line at Paya Lebar. One of Singapore's busiest interchange stations — everyone rushing, phones out. Me? No rush. I was just a retiree heading for lor mee. 前往老机场路美食中心的途中，我在巴耶利峇换乘了环线。这是新加坡最繁忙的转换站之一——人来人往，人手一机。而我？不慌不忙。只是一个退休人士，去吃一碗卤面。

Photo 5 of 5 — The Famous Lor Mee Stall (Closed!)第5张，共5张——著名卤面摊（休息中！）

Xin Mei Xiang Zheng Zong Lor Mee — one of Old Airport Road's most famous stalls. Shutter down. Off day. I had walked all the way there for lor mee, and the lor mee was not available. Such is life. Even retirement has its surprises. 新美香正宗卤面——老机场路最著名的摊位之一。卷帘门拉下，休息一天。我大老远跑来吃卤面，结果卤面没得吃。这就是生活。就连退休，也有它的意外。
