When to See a Doctor何时应就医

If any symptom stays "severe" for more than a few days, or several pain points show up together (e.g. low energy + poor appetite + more rest needed), that combination is worth mentioning to a doctor — don't wait it out alone. 如果任何症状持续"严重"超过几天，或多个不适同时出现（例如精力下降+食欲不振+需要更多休息），这种组合值得告诉医生 — 不要一个人硬扛。

Back pain that keeps recurring — especially triggered by the same movements (squats, bending, lifting) — is a sign the back isn't fully recovered yet. Worth seeing a doctor or physiotherapist for a proper assessment rather than just resting it off each time. 背痛反复发作——尤其是被同样的动作（深蹲、弯腰、提重物）引发——说明背部还没有完全恢复。建议看医生或物理治疗师做正式检查，而不是每次都只靠休息了事。
