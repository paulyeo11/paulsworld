All Corrections — Teacher's Notes

✏️ Teacher's Corrections

1
Informal Formatting
(Job Description): → Job Description:
Remove the parentheses. They look informal and unprofessional on a LinkedIn job post.
2
Inconsistent Word (Post vs. Graphic)
team performance analyses → team performance analysis
The graphic already uses "analysis" (singular). Keep both the same — use "analysis".
3
Grammar Error — Wrong Verb Tense
Please sent your CV → Please send your CV
"Sent" is past tense. "Send" is the correct command form. This error appears in BOTH the post text and the graphic.
4
Grammar Error — Wrong Word Form
your update CV → your updated CV
"Update" is a verb. You need the adjective form "updated". Appears in BOTH the post and graphic.
5
Unclear Subject Line Format
Subject : name_position → Subject: [Your Full Name] – Head of Sales
The underscore format is confusing. Give a clear example so candidates know exactly what to write.
6
Wrong Title in Graphic — Missing Word
HEAD SALES → HEAD OF SALES
Missing the word "OF". The post text says "Head of Sales" correctly, but the banner graphic leaves it out. The graphic needs to be remade.
7
Inconsistent Capitalization in Graphic
HVAC Filter / COMPRESSOR / DIESEL ENGINE → HVAC Filter / Compressor / Diesel Engine
COMPRESSOR and DIESEL ENGINE are all-caps but HVAC Filter is not. Use the same style throughout.
8
Wrong Abbreviation in Graphic
Ms. Office → MS Office
"Ms." is an honorific for women (like Mrs. or Mr.). The correct abbreviation for Microsoft is "MS" — no dot, capital S.
