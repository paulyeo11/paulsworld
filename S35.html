Back to Niseko 重返二世谷

A song for Joanne, written after we returned to Niseko for the first time since 2023 — peaceful roads, good food, and the happiest kind of quiet 写给Joanne的一首歌——自2023年后，我们第一次重返二世谷，平静的道路、美味的食物，还有最幸福的那份宁静

By Paul Yeo  ·  Niseko, Japan  ·  July 2026 杨汉忠 著  ·  日本二世谷  ·  2026年7月

✅ Link copied!链接已复制！

Going back to Niseko after 2023, I didn't expect it to feel this good. The same road, but somehow more peaceful. The same kind of food, but somehow it tasted even better. Mostly, it was the simple happiness of spending unhurried time with my wife — no rush, no schedule, just the two of us. So instead of just writing it down, I turned it into a song. 2023年之后再回二世谷，我没想到会感觉这么好。还是那条路，却莫名地更加平静。还是那样的食物，却莫名地更加美味。而最主要的，是与妻子一起悠闲度过时光的那份简单的幸福——不赶时间，没有行程表，只有我们两个人。所以我没有只是把它写下来，而是把它写成了一首歌。

Key of G major, upbeat tempo (~110 bpm). Simple strum: down-down-up-up-down-up. Capo 2 if you want it brighter and higher for singing. G大调，欢快节奏（约110拍/分钟）。简单扫弦：下-下-上-上-下-上。若想唱得更明亮更高，可夹二品变调夹。

How to Sing It — A Melody Guide如何演唱——旋律指引

I can't sing this for you, but here's a simple melody shape to hum along to — the note names follow the G major scale (G A B C D E F#). Treat this as a starting point, not a strict script; let it settle into whatever feels natural when you hum it out loud. 我没办法唱给你听，但这里有一个简单的旋律走向可以哼唱——音名按照G大调音阶（G A B C D E F#）。请把这当作一个起点，而不是严格的谱子；大声哼出来，找到感觉自然的走向即可。
