🚗 Hokkaido Car Rental — E-Motion ▾
Hokkaido-focused, accepts SGD bank transfer, clear weekly/monthly rates. For 17–18 days ask for monthly rate. Booking 21 days (3 weeks) and returning early is likely cheaper than daily rates for 18 days.
🗺️ Getting Around Hokkaido ▾
Niseko to New Chitose Airport: ~2 hours by car (Route 230 → expressway).
Fuel: Regular gasoline (レギュラー). Fill up in towns — rural areas have fewer stations.
Driving: Left side of road. Speed limit 60–80 km/h on rural roads. Watch for deer/fox at night.
📱 Connectivity & Navigation ▾
Get a Japan SIM card or pocket Wi-Fi at Narita on arrival.
Maps: Google Maps works well. Download offline Hokkaido maps before leaving Tokyo.
Suica card: Useful for trains and convenience stores in Tokyo.
