H4: My Walking Habit — 10,000 Steps a Day

How a Simple Daily Walk Transformed My Health at 70简单的日常散步如何70岁改变了我的健康

How It Started这一切是怎么开始的

It began simply: my doctor said "walk more." I started with 20 minutes around the neighbourhood. Within a week, I was hooked. Within a month, I was doing 10,000 steps every single day without fail.

Walking has become the cornerstone of my health routine at 70. It costs nothing, needs no gym, and the benefits are extraordinary.

The Science of Walking步行的科学

For the Heart Regular walking lowers blood pressure, reduces LDL cholesterol, and strengthens the heart muscle.
For the Mind Walking releases endorphins, reduces cortisol (stress hormone), and lowers risk of dementia.
For the Body Strengthens bones and muscles, improves balance and coordination, helps maintain healthy weight.

My Walking Routine我的步行日常

6:00 AM
Morning devotion and light stretching before walking
6:30 AM
45-minute brisk walk around the neighbourhood or Bishan Park
4:00 PM
Afternoon walk if morning steps were insufficient
Evening
Short walk after dinner to aid digestion

My Results After 12 Months12个月后的成果

📈 Health Improvements

  • Lost 4kg without changing my diet significantly
  • Blood pressure improved from 145/90 to 128/82
  • Resting heart rate dropped from 78 to 68 bpm
  • Sleep quality improved dramatically
  • Energy levels throughout the day are much higher
  • Mood and mental clarity noticeably better

Tips for Seniors Who Want to Start

If you haven't walked regularly before, start small. Even 10 minutes a day is a great beginning. The key is consistency — not speed, not distance, just daily movement.

Get a good pair of shoes, track your steps with your phone or a wearable, and find a route that you enjoy. The habit will build itself.
