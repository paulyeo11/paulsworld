🌧️ Why Day 4, not Day 3: Day 3's rain risk was too high for a mostly-outdoor theme park with long open-air queues. Asakusa (Day 3) is more rain-friendly — Nakamise-dori is a covered arcade and Senso-ji's main hall is indoor. DisneySea got pushed to Day 4 to give the weather a better chance to clear.为什么改到第四天：第三天下雨风险较高，不适合大部分露天、排队区多为户外的主题公园。浅草（第三天）更适合雨天 — 仲见世通是有顶棚的商店街，浅草寺本堂也在室内。因此把海洋迪士尼改到第四天，给天气多一天转晴的机会。
🎫 Before You Go出发前须知
Tokyo Disney Resort tickets are date-specific and sell out — book online in advance via the official Tokyo Disney Resort app or site, not at the gate. Double-check the day's operating hours the night before, since they vary by date and sometimes include special evening events. 东京迪士尼门票是指定日期的，且可能售罄 — 请提前通过官方东京迪士尼度假区App或官网预订，不要到现场购票。出发前一晚请再次确认当天的开园时间，因为不同日期会有变化，有时还包含特别夜间活动。

A rocky start, saved by a stranger一波三折的开始，幸得陌生人相助
On the way to Tokyo DisneySea this morning, ran into a stretch of bad luck all at once — the IC transport card had run dry, the DBS Visa card's PIN wasn't set up yet, the ATM wouldn't dispense cash, and the backup card wasn't even on hand. All that was needed was 10 SGD for the bus fare. A kind stranger stepped in without hesitation and covered it — a small act of kindness that turned a stressful morning into one worth remembering. Ticket in hand and QR code confirmed valid, the gates opened without any further trouble. First stop: Journey to the Center of the Earth — joined the queue at 9:45 AM only to find a 2-hour wait ahead. A long stand for a 70-year-old, but the ride's reputation made it worth holding the spot. The ride finally started at 12:05 PM — right on the 2-hour mark.今天早上前往东京迪士尼海洋的路上接连遇到麻烦——交通卡余额不足，DBS Visa卡还没设置PIN码，ATM机也无法取现，备用卡又不在身边。当时只差新币10元的巴士车费。一位好心的陌生人二话不说主动帮忙解围——一个小小的善举，让原本紧张的早晨变成了难忘的一刻。门票在手，QR码确认有效，顺利通过闸门入园。第一站：地心探险之旅——早上9:45排队时才发现要等足足2小时。对70岁的人来说是场不小的考验，但这个项目的口碑让这份等待显得值得。游乐设施终于在中午12:05启动——正好卡在两小时的等待时长上。
