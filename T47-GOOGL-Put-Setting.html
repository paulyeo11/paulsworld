Can I Close Early to Avoid Assignment?能否提前平仓以避免被行权？

Yes. You can buy to close a short put any time before expiration (market hours, if there's a buyer). Closing removes the obligation entirely — once it's closed, you can't be assigned.可以。在到期前的任何交易时段（只要有买方），都可以买回平仓已卖出的认沽期权。平仓即完全解除义务——一旦平仓，就不会被行权。

  • You don't keep the full premium if you close early — you already collected it all when you sold. Profit = premium collected minus the price you pay to buy it back now.提前平仓无法保留全部权利金——卖出时已全额收取。盈利＝收取的权利金减去现在买回的成本。
  • As the share price drops closer to the strike, the put costs more to buy back, so profit shrinks — and if it's moved far enough, closing could even lock in a loss instead of a profit.股价越接近行使价，买回成本越高，盈利也随之缩水——若跌幅够大，平仓甚至可能锁定亏损而非盈利。
  • Early assignment (US-style options) is technically possible whenever a put is in-the-money, but in practice it's rare until close to expiration.美式期权理论上只要认沽期权处于价内即可能被提前行权，但实务上通常要接近到期日才会发生。
