📋 Day 3 Plan第三天计划
☀️
Morning早上
Wake up in Kamata在蒲田醒来
Third morning based at BON Tokyo Kamata Parkside — breakfast, then head out for old Tokyo.在BON东京蒲田Parkside的第三个早晨 — 用过早餐后出发前往老东京。
🚃
Late morning上午晚些时候
Train to Asakusa乘车前往浅草
Keikyu Line → Sengakuji, transfer to the Toei Asakusa Line straight through to Asakusa Station. No changes at busy Shinagawa/Shibuya-style hubs — a calmer ride than Day 2.京急线 → 泉岳寺，转乘都营浅草线直达浅草站。不需要在品川/涩谷那样繁忙的枢纽换乘 — 比第二天更轻松。
⛩️
Arrive抵达
Kaminarimon Gate雷门
The giant red lantern marking the entrance to Senso-ji — Tokyo's oldest temple, founded 645 AD.巨大的红灯笼标志着浅草寺的入口 — 东京最古老的寺庙，始建于公元645年。
🛍️
Late morning上午晚些时候
Walk Nakamise-dori漫步仲见世通
Slow stroll down the ~250 m shopping street to the temple — senbei, souvenirs, yukata, ningyo-yaki stalls the whole way. Flat, easy pace, lots of benches nearby.沿约250米的仲见世商店街慢慢走到寺庙 — 一路都是仙贝、纪念品、浴衣、人形烧摊位。路面平坦，节奏轻松，附近长椅很多。
🍜
Lunch午餐
Lunch near Senso-ji浅草寺附近用午餐
Plenty of small tempura and unagi restaurants tucked around the temple grounds and side streets.寺庙周边和小巷里有不少天妇罗和鳗鱼小店。
🌉
Afternoon下午
Sumida River & Skytree view隅田川与晴空塔景观
Short walk to the riverbank near Azumabashi Bridge for a clear view of Tokyo Skytree and the golden Asahi Beer flame sculpture across the water.步行至吾妻桥附近的河岸，可清楚看到东京晴空塔和河对岸金色的朝日啤酒火焰雕塑。
🌃
Evening晚上
Back to Kamata返回蒲田
Retrace the route via Sengakuji back to the hotel. Rest up for Day 4.经泉岳寺原路返回酒店，为第四天休息。

🚶 Walk — Kaminarimon to Sumida River步行 — 雷门至隅田川
🚶 On foot · ~25–30 min · ~1.2 km · flat & easy步行 · 约25-30分钟 · 约1.2公里 · 平坦轻松
1
Kaminarimon Gate → Nakamise-dori雷门 → 仲见世通
Pass under the giant red lantern, then straight down the shopping street toward the temple. 穿过巨大的红灯笼，沿商店街直行前往寺庙。
2
Senso-ji Main Hall & five-storied pagoda浅草寺本堂与五重塔
Through Hozomon Gate to the main hall — incense burner out front, pagoda just to the west. 穿过宝藏门到本堂 — 前方有香炉，五重塔在西侧不远处。
3
Azumabashi Bridge → Sumida River view吾妻桥 → 隅田川景观
East along Kaminarimon-dori to the river — Tokyo Skytree and the golden Asahi Beer flame sculpture across the water. 沿雷门通向东走到河边 — 河对岸可见东京晴空塔和金色的朝日啤酒火焰雕塑。
