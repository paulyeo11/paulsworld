Retired at 67, baptised at 70 — I want the next 20 years to be my fullest chapter yet: healthy enough to keep adventuring, close enough to Joanne and family to matter every day, curious enough to keep learning, and grounded enough in faith to face whatever comes with peace.

67岁退休，70岁受洗 — 我希望未来的20年，是我人生中最丰盛的一个篇章：身体健康到还能继续冒险，与Joanne和家人足够亲近到每天都有意义，保持好奇心持续学习，也在信仰上足够扎实，能以平静的心面对一切。

This plan is split into the Next 5 Years (age 70–75) where I have the most energy and mobility, and a Long-Term Plan (years 6–20, age 76–90) which carries me through an active middle stretch into my golden years. The goal is never just "more years" — it's more good years.

这份计划分为未来5年（70–75岁），这是我体力和行动力最充沛的阶段；以及长期计划（第6至20年，76–90岁），涵盖从活跃的中段一直到晚年的黄金岁月。目标从来不是单纯"活得更久"，而是"活得更好"。
