✅ What Makes a Good Iron Condor Candidate✅ 铁鹰策略优质标的的条件

  • High options liquidity期权流动性高 — tight bid/ask spread, high open interest & volume on the strikes you want买卖价差窄，目标行使价的未平仓量与成交量高
  • Range-bound / no strong trend区间震荡，无明显趋势 — a stock grinding sideways is far safer than one trending hard in one direction横向震荡的股票远比单边强势趋势的股票安全
  • Moderate-to-high IV Rank中至高隐含波动率排名（IV Rank） — roughly 30–70%+, so the premium collected is worth the risk大致30%–70%以上，让收取的权利金值得承担的风险
  • No earnings/major event before expiry到期前无财报或重大事件 — avoid earnings week unless you're deliberately trading the earnings move除非刻意针对财报行情交易，否则应避开财报周
  • 30–45 DTE sweet spot30–45天到期为佳 — enough premium and theta decay without excessive gamma risk near expiry权利金与时间价值衰减适中，同时避免临近到期的伽玛风险
  • Avoid low-priced / meme-prone names避免低价股或炒作性个股 — erratic, news-driven stocks can blow through both wings quickly受消息面驱动、走势不稳的股票可能迅速突破两侧翼
