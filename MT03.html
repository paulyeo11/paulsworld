Kanas Lake ⭐ + Hemu Village ⭐
4–6 Sep · 2 nights · Peak autumn season — book early!
🗺️ Kanas Map
⚠️ Book via Trip.com — No Booking.com coverage here
Kanas & Hemu are extremely remote. Accommodation = wooden log guesthouses (木屋), yurts (蒙古包) & local inns. September is PEAK season — book 2–3 months ahead.
Look for "喀纳斯景区" hotels. Budget RMB 200–400/room. Confirm they accept foreign passport holders.
