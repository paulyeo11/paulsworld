A Healthy Pace健康的节奏

A sustainable, healthy pace of change is roughly 0.25–0.5 kg per week. Faster swings are often water weight, not real progress — don't chase the daily number, watch the trend over several weeks. If weight moves sharply without trying, or stalls for months despite consistent effort, that's worth mentioning to a doctor. 可持续且健康的变化节奏大约是每周0.25至0.5公斤。变化过快通常只是水分波动，并非真正的进展 — 不必纠结每天的数字，应观察几周的整体趋势。若体重在没有刻意努力下大幅变动，或持续努力数月仍毫无变化，这值得告诉医生。
