T49: Coin Toss & Trading — Why Small Samples LieT49：抛硬币与交易——为何小样本会骗人

Toss a coin a few times and it looks unfair. Toss it thousands of times and it settles at 50/50. Trading results behave exactly the same way — and the fix isn't a better win rate, it's a better payoff ratio.抛几次硬币，结果看起来不公平；抛几千次，结果会稳定在50/50。交易的结果表现完全一样——解决办法不是提高胜率，而是提高盈亏比。

The Coin Toss Analogy抛硬币的比喻

Toss a coin: heads or tails, 50/50. But toss it only 5 or 10 times and the results often look nothing like 50/50 — you might get 4 heads in a row, or 7 tails out of 10. That's not a broken coin. It's just a small sample.抛一枚硬币：正面或反面，各50%。但如果只抛5次或10次，结果往往看起来完全不像50/50——你可能连续抛出4次正面，或10次里有7次反面。这不是硬币坏了，只是样本太小。

Toss the same coin 1,000 or 10,000 times and the ratio pulls back toward 50/50, tighter every time. This is the Law of Large Numbers: individual outcomes are random, but the average of many outcomes converges on the true probability.同一枚硬币抛1,000次或10,000次，比例会逐渐拉回接近50/50，且越抛越稳定。这就是大数法则：单次结果是随机的，但大量结果的平均值会趋近于真实概率。

The Trading Parallel交易中的对照

Trading results follow the exact same shape:交易结果的形态一模一样：

  • Small sample (a handful of trades): losing streaks happen even with a genuinely good strategy — 3 or 4 losses in a row can look like proof the strategy is broken, the same way 4 tails in a row doesn't mean the coin is broken.小样本（几笔交易）：即使策略本身很好，连续亏损也会发生——连续3、4笔亏损看起来像是策略失效的证据，就像连续4次反面不代表硬币有问题一样。
  • Large sample (dozens/hundreds of trades): the true win rate and true edge reveal themselves. A handful of open Iron Condors (T29) is still a small sample — no single trade, win or loss, should change the underlying rules.大样本（几十／几百笔交易）：真实胜率与真实优势才会显现出来。目前持有的几个铁鹰仓位（T29）仍属于小样本——任何单笔交易的输赢，都不该改变原本的交易法则。

This is exactly why T34 ("3 Years of Options Trading") matters more as a track record than any single week's P&L — 3 years is a big enough sample for the real edge to show through the noise.这也正是为何T34（「3年期权交易记录」）作为长期记录比任一周的盈亏更重要——3年是足够大的样本，能让真实优势穿透噪音显现出来。

The rule that makes small-sample noise survivable让小样本噪音也能撑过去的法则
Keep each win bigger than each loss — do that consistently, and you can be profitable even without a high win rate让每次盈利大于每次亏损——只要持续做到，即使胜率不高也能获利

Why the Payoff Ratio Matters More Than Win Rate为何盈亏比比胜率更重要

A strategy's long-run result isn't decided by win rate alone — it's decided by expectancy: win rate multiplied by average win, minus loss rate multiplied by average loss.策略的长期结果并非只由胜率决定——而是由期望值决定：胜率乘以平均盈利，减去败率乘以平均亏损。

Expectancy (E) = [Win Rate (W) × Avg Win (R)] − [(1 − W) × Avg Loss (L)]

✅ Both scenarios are profitable — a 40% win rate still wins long-run if the average win stays bigger than the average loss.两种情境都能获利——只要平均盈利持续大于平均亏损，即使胜率只有40%，长期仍会盈利。

The danger case is the opposite of Scenario B: a high win rate propped up by "small wins, occasional huge loss" — a handful of quiet months can be erased by one loss that breaks the ratio. That's the exact discipline behind the DTE 15 exit rule in T31: cut a losing Iron Condor before the loss grows past what the wins can cover.真正危险的是Scenario B的反面：靠「小赢、偶尔巨亏」撑起来的高胜率——几个月的平稳获利可能被一次打破比例的巨大亏损全部抹平。这正是T31中DTE15离场法则背后的纪律：在亏损扩大到盈利无法弥补之前，先平掉亏损的铁鹰仓位。

Takeaway要点总结

  • Few tosses / few trades → results look uneven, streaks feel meaningful. They usually aren't.少量抛掷／少量交易 → 结果看起来不均衡，连胜连亏感觉很有意义。通常并非如此。
  • Many tosses / many trades → the true probability and true edge come through.大量抛掷／大量交易 → 真实概率与真实优势才会显现。
  • Keep each win bigger than each loss, do it consistently, and the strategy stays profitable over time — even through the losing streaks a small sample will always contain.让每次盈利大于每次亏损，并持续做到——即使小样本中必然出现的连续亏损也不影响策略长期获利。
