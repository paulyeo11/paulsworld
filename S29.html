This is where today's walk begins — the swimming pool at ECO Sanctuary, my new home. The pool sits between the two towers, catching the morning light. I left my towel on the lounger and set off to explore the neighbourhood.

今天的漫步从这里开始——我的新家ECO Sanctuary的游泳池。泳池夹在两座高楼之间，沐浴着晨光。我把毛巾放在躺椅上，便出发探索社区了。

The Rail Mall — a neighbourhood strip mall built along the old Keretapi Tanah Melayu (KTM) railway corridor. I stopped here for a bowl of home-made noodles (家常好吃面粉粿) — hand-made dry noodles, the kind you only find at a good old-school stall. The neon signs glow warm against the night sky.

The Rail Mall——一座沿旧马来亚铁路（KTM）走廊建造的社区商铺。我在这里停下来吃了一碗家常好吃面粉粿——手工干捞面，这种味道只有在老派好摊子才能找到。霓虹灯在夜空中散发着温暖的光芒。
