Why This Matters for an IC为什么这对铁鹰策略很重要

A normal-DTE Iron Condor opened tonight would still be open when META reports on 29 Jul — unlike the current SPY IC on T29, which has no single-stock earnings event to worry about. Earnings moves regularly gap through both short strikes of a standard-width condor, turning a defined "max loss" into a near-certain one.今晚若开一笔标准到期日的铁鹰仓位，到7月29日META公布财报时仍会持仓中——这与目前T29上的SPY铁鹰仓位不同，SPY没有单一股票的财报事件需要担心。财报公布后的价格跳空经常会同时穿越标准宽度铁鹰的两侧卖出行权价，让本应「有限」的最大亏损变成几乎必然发生的亏损。

The IV rank ~100 reading looks tempting — rich premium to sell — but that richness exists because the market is pricing in the earnings gap risk, not because the options are mispriced. Selling into it without accounting for the binary event is picking up pennies in front of a steamroller.约100的隐含波动率排名看起来很诱人——权利金丰厚——但这份「丰厚」正是因为市场已经把财报跳空风险计入价格，而不是期权定价错误。若不考虑这个二元事件就卖出期权，等于是在压路机前捡便士。

⚠️ Rich IV before earnings is a trap for standard-DTE premium selling, not a green light.财报前的高隐含波动率，对标准到期日的卖权策略来说是陷阱，而不是绿灯。
