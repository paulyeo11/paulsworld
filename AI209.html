🌍 The Gap Is Growing — Fast🌍 差距正在扩大——很快

The speaker has spent over 20 years in tech and AI as a CEO, board member and investor. He opens with a warning that stopped me in my tracks: the gap between people who understand AI and those who don't is getting wider every single day.

💡 Key Insight

AI tools like ChatGPT don't actually understand your language — they predict it. They are sophisticated autocomplete machines. Once you understand this, everything about how to use AI well becomes clear.

📅 The 4-Week Roadmap📅 四周路线图

The video lays out a clear week-by-week plan. Here is what each week focuses on:

Week 1

🗣️ Learn Machine English

Stop talking to AI like a person. Learn to write sharp, structured prompts using the AIM framework — Actor, Input, Mission. Vague prompts give vague answers. Sharp prompts give sharp results.

Week 2

🎸 Pick One Tool, Go Deep

Don't jump between 10 AI tools. Pick one — Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini — and master it completely. Like learning a musical instrument, depth beats breadth every time.

Week 3

🔁 Build Your AI Workflow

By week three, start integrating AI into your daily routine. Use it for real tasks — writing, research, planning, problem-solving. Build repeatable workflows that save you hours every week.

Week 4

🚀 Go Beyond Prompting

Learn the MAAP framework to structure complex thinking with AI. Move from using AI as a tool to using it as a thinking partner. This is where you join the top 1%.

🎯 The AIM Framework🎯 AIM框架

The most immediately useful thing in the video is the AIM framework for writing better prompts. Instead of typing "fix my resume," you structure your request like this:

🎯 AIM — The 3-Part Prompt Structure

A
Actor — Tell AI who it's playing "You are the world's best résumé editor with 20 years of experience..."
I
Input — Give it the context and data "Here is my current résumé and the job description I'm applying for..."
M
Mission — Tell it exactly what to do "Give me 10 specific improvements to make my résumé stand out for this role."

The speaker says using AIM makes your results 5 to 10 times better immediately. I tried it myself and he is absolutely right. The difference is remarkable.
