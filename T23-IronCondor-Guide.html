What Is a 4-Leg Iron Condor?什么是四腿铁鹰策略？

An Iron Condor is a defined-risk, income-generating options trade built from 4 legs — two put options and two call options, all on the same underlying and the same expiry date. You sell a put spread below the current price and a call spread above it, betting the stock stays inside that range until expiry. You collect a net credit up front; the most you can lose is capped by the "wings" you buy for protection.铁鹰策略是一种风险明确、以收取权利金为目的的期权交易，由4条腿组成——两个认沽（put）期权与两个认购（call）期权，标的与到期日相同。你在现价下方卖出一个认沽价差，在现价上方卖出一个认购价差，赌股价在到期前维持在该区间内。你会先收到一笔净权利金；最大亏损则由你买入作为保护的"翼"（wing）所限制。

Leg腿位Action操作Purpose作用
Long Put买入认沽Buy 1 put, lower strike买入1张认沽，行使价较低Caps loss on the downside限制下跌方向的亏损
Short Put卖出认沽Sell 1 put, higher strike卖出1张认沽，行使价较高Collects premium; your downside "line in the sand"收取权利金；下方的防守线
Short Call卖出认购Sell 1 call, lower strike卖出1张认购，行使价较低Collects premium; your upside "line in the sand"收取权利金；上方的防守线
Long Call买入认购Buy 1 call, higher strike买入1张认购，行使价较高Caps loss on the upside限制上涨方向的亏损

The two short strikes (short put + short call) form the "body" — where you want price to stay. The two long strikes (long put + long call) are the "wings" — pure insurance that define your worst case.两个短腿（卖出认沽＋卖出认购）构成策略的"主体"——即你希望价格停留的区间。两个长腿（买入认沽＋买入认购）则是"翼"——纯粹作为保险，界定你的最坏情况。

✅ Entry Checklist✅ 入场检查清单

  • IV Rank 30–70%+IV排名30%–70%以上 — enough implied volatility that the credit is worth the risk. Check on your broker's option chain, not this site.隐含波动率足够高，使收取的权利金值得承担的风险。请在券商的期权链上查看，而非本网站。
  • 30–45 DTE30–45天到期 — the classic sweet spot for theta decay vs. gamma risk时间价值衰减与伽玛风险之间的经典甜蜜点
  • Short strikes ≈ 0.16–0.20 delta短腿约0.16–0.20 delta — roughly a 1-standard-deviation move, ~70–85% probability both shorts expire worthless大致相当于1个标准差的波动，短腿到期归零的概率约70%–85%
  • No earnings/major event before expiry到期前无财报或重大事件 — check the earnings calendar; a surprise gap can blow through both wings请核对财报日历；意外跳空可能同时突破两侧翼
  • Credit ≥ ⅓ of wing width权利金 ≥ 翼距的三分之一 — e.g. a $10 wing should collect roughly $3+ in credit, or the risk/reward isn't worth it例如10美元的翼距，应至少收取约3美元以上的权利金，否则风险回报比不划算
  • Liquid option chain期权链流动性充足 — tight bid/ask spread, decent open interest at your strikes买卖价差窄，目标行使价的未平仓量充足
  • Position sized to your account仓位与账户规模匹配 — max loss on this one trade should be a small slice of total capital (see Position Sizing below)单笔交易的最大亏损应只占总资金的一小部分（见下方"仓位规模"）

Position Sizing & Risk Management仓位规模与风险管理

Max loss on an Iron Condor is fixed and known before you enter — that's the whole point of buying the wings. Work backwards from your account size, not forwards from "how many contracts can I afford."铁鹰策略的最大亏损在入场前就已固定且已知——这正是买入翼腿的意义所在。请从账户规模倒推仓位，而不是从"我能负担几张合约"正向推算。

Rule of thumb经验法则
Risk 1–5% of account per trade
Contracts合约数量
= (Account × risk%) ÷ (Max Loss per contract × 100)

If several Iron Condors are open at once, keep an eye on total portfolio delta and total capital at risk across all of them combined — not just each trade in isolation.若同时持有多笔铁鹰仓位，请留意所有仓位加总后的组合Delta与总风险资本——而不仅仅是单笔交易本身。

Trade Management & Exit Rules交易管理与离场规则

🎯
Take profit at 50%达到50%即获利了结
A common rule: close the trade once you've captured about half the max profit, rather than waiting greedily for 100%. Frees capital and cuts tail risk.常见做法：一旦获利达到最大盈利的约一半，即平仓，而非贪心地等待100%。这样能释放资金并降低尾部风险。
⏳
Manage or close by 21 DTE21天到期前处理或平仓
Gamma risk accelerates in the final weeks. Many traders close, roll, or reduce size around 21 days to expiry regardless of profit level.到期前的最后几周伽玛风险会加速上升。许多交易者会在到期前约21天，不论盈亏都选择平仓、展期或缩减仓位。
🛑
Stop loss ≈ 2× credit received止损约为所收权利金的2倍
A common defined-risk rule: if the trade's loss reaches roughly 2× the credit you collected, close it rather than hoping for a reversal.常见的风险管理规则：若亏损达到所收权利金的约2倍，应平仓止损，而非寄望于反转。
🔄
Roll a tested side展期被测试的一侧
If price approaches one short strike but the thesis (range-bound) still holds, some traders roll that side out in time and/or further away in strike for a fresh credit.若价格接近某一短腿行使价，但"区间震荡"的判断依然成立，部分交易者会将该侧展期至更远的到期日和/或行使价，重新收取权利金。
🚪
Exit if the thesis breaks判断失效即离场
If the stock starts trending hard and breaks out of the band with volume/news behind it, don't wait for the stop loss number — the range-bound thesis is already wrong.若股价开始出现明显趋势，并伴随成交量或消息面突破区间，不必等到止损点位——"区间震荡"的前提已经不成立。

⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid⚠️ 常见错误

  • Trading through earnings by accident无意间跨过财报日交易 — always check the earnings date before entry, not after入场前务必核对财报日期，而非事后才发现
  • Strikes too tight for the premium行使价过窄以换取更多权利金 — chasing extra credit by placing shorts too close to price dramatically raises the odds of a breach为多收权利金而将短腿设得离现价太近，会大幅提高被突破的概率
  • No wings ("naked strangle" by mistake)忘记买翼（误变成裸卖宽跨式） — forgetting to buy the protective long put/call turns a defined-risk trade into unlimited risk忘记买入保护性的认沽/认购，会让风险明确的策略变成无限风险
  • Oversized position仓位过大 — one bad trade shouldn't threaten the account — size by max loss, not by how much credit "feels" worth collecting一笔失败的交易不应威胁整个账户——应按最大亏损来定仓位，而非按"感觉值得收多少权利金"来定
  • Ignoring liquidity忽视流动性 — wide bid/ask spreads on illiquid strikes quietly eat into both entry and exit prices流动性差的行使价买卖价差宽，会在进场与出场时悄悄侵蚀收益
  • Holding too close to expiry持仓至过于接近到期日 — gamma risk near expiry means small price moves cause outsized P&L swings临近到期时伽玛风险升高，微小的价格变动也会造成损益的大幅波动
