Holy Word for Morning Revival — Week 1, Day 1

Monday 25 May 2026 · 2026 ICSC
Big Idea: God does not just want to bless you — He wants to live inside you and fill you with Himself.
1
🌊 God flows into you like a river
Father = source · Jesus = richness · Spirit = river. Let God saturate you every morning.
2
📖 The Bible has one message
Every story in Scripture points to this: God wants to give you Himself — not just blessings.
3
🌈 God never breaks His word
The rainbow = His faithfulness. When you feel weak, say: "Lord, make me strong!"
4
✝️ In Christ, God sees you as perfect
You don't need to perform. In Christ, God sees His own righteousness, holiness and glory.
5
🌟 People should sense God when they meet you
When filled with Christ, His qualities shine out naturally in your daily life — even angels notice.
💡 Carry This Through Monday
"Lord, I am weak — but You are faithful. Fill me with Yourself today."
