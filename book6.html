Holy Word for Morning Revival
Week 1 · Day 1 · Monday 25 May

2026 ICSC · 5 Key Truths in Simple Words

Big Idea: God does not just want to bless you — He wants to live inside you and fill you with Himself.
1
🌊
God flows into you like a river
The Father is the source, Jesus is the richness, the Spirit is the river. Every morning with God is letting the Triune God saturate your whole being — not just reading words.
2
📖
The whole Bible has one message
From Genesis to Revelation, every story points to the same thing: God wants to give you Himself — not just blessings, but His own life and nature living inside you.
3
🌈
The rainbow — God never breaks His word
Blue = His righteousness. Red = His holiness. Yellow = His glory. When life is stormy, look up. God is faithful. Tell Him: "I am weak — make me strong according to Your word."
4
✝️
In Christ, God sees you as perfect
You do not need to perform for God. Because you are in Christ, when God looks at you He sees Jesus — righteousness, holiness and glory. Just stay in Him.
5
🌟
People around you should sense God when they meet you
When you are filled with Christ, His qualities naturally shine out — at home, with family, with strangers. The angels notice. Every believer is meant to be a rainbow testimony of God in the world.
💡 Carry This Through Monday
"Lord, I am weak — but You are faithful.
Fill me with Yourself today."
