🌅

Morning Nourishment

Dan. 10:5-7

I lifted up my eyes and I looked, and there was a certain man, clothed in linen, whose loins were girded with the fine gold of Uphaz. His body also was like beryl, His face like the appearance of lightning, His eyes like torches of fire, His arms and His feet like the gleam of polished bronze, and the sound of His words like the sound of a multitude.

Daniel saw the excellent Christ — clothed in linen (His humanity as Priest), girded with fine gold (His divine kingship), His body like beryl (precious and full of life), His face like lightning (His brightness), His eyes like fire (searching and judging), His arms and feet like bronze (tested and proven), and His words like a multitude (powerful and authoritative).

Before showing Daniel the great distress ahead, God first showed him the excellent man of Daniel 10. Christ appeared first — for Daniel's appreciation, consolation, encouragement, and stabilization. God always shows you Christ before He shows you the storm.

Only Daniel saw the vision. The others with him could not see it — they relied on physical sight. The vision of Christ is spiritual. In the world's eyes, Jesus is merely a historical figure. But under God's mercy, with a spiritual view, we see how dear and precious Christ truly is.

📖 Today's Reading

Christ appeared to Daniel in His priesthood — dressed in linen, the priestly garment. He was not in armour for fighting but in the robe of a priest caring for God's captive people. Even while Israel was in Babylon, Christ was their Priest, taking care of them.

Christ also appeared in His kingship — the golden girdle. His priesthood is human; His kingship is divine. He serves us in His humanity and rules over all in His divinity. He is both our caring Priest and our sovereign King at the same time.

Finally, Christ appeared in the sound of His words — like the sound of a multitude. He is the One who speaks with authority over everything. Today, when you read the Bible, when you hear His word — that is the sound of the multitude. His speaking is with all authority. How precious He is!

Lord Jesus, let me see You as Daniel saw You — excellent, precious, bright and near. Be my Priest today. Rule as my King. Open my eyes to see You.

Big Idea: The excellent Christ — your caring Priest, your sovereign King, your bright and precious Lord.
1
👗
Christ is your Priest in linen

He is not standing far away judging you. He is dressed as a Priest, right beside you, taking care of you — even in your Babylon, even in your darkest season.

2
👑
Christ is your King in gold

His priesthood is human — He serves you gently. His kingship is divine — He rules over everything around you. You have both at the same time.

3
⚡
Christ is brighter than lightning

His face is like lightning, His eyes like fire — He sees everything, searches everything. Nothing is hidden from Him. This is not scary — it is safe.

4
🔥
He was tested so He could test

His feet are like polished bronze — He was tried and judged by God, and came out bright. He knows what it is to be tested. He walks with you in yours.

5
👁️
Intimacy unlocks the vision

Daniel saw the vision — others standing with him could not. Only those intimate with God see what others miss. Closeness to God opens spiritual eyes.
