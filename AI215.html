Two years ago, when I decided to learn AI, my dream was simple — I wanted an AI assistant to help me plan my travels. But I failed. The technology simply wasn’t mature enough. Yet now, in just one month, my life has completely changed.

两年前，当我决定学习AI的时候，我的梦想很简单——我想要一個AI助手来帮我规划旅行。结果呢？我失败了。因为那时候的技术还不够成熟。但是现在，仍仅一個月的时间里，我的生活已经完全改变了。

I subscribe to Claude AI for just $20 a month. My approach is unique: work one hour, rest four. Work one hour, rest four. Why? Because AI does so much for me that I simply don’t need to work all the time. 我订钓Claude AI，每個月只要20美元。我的使用方式很特别——我工作一小时，然后休息四小时。工作一小时，休息四小时。为什么？因为AI能帮我做的事情太多了，所以我不需要一直工作。

😂 The Moment That Surprised Me😂 让我惨喜的那一反

Once, I even tested AI by telling it my wife was using the computer. You know what happened? It never gave up on me. Never. Even when my questions were strange, even when I asked unreasonable things, it politely declined and gave me better suggestions instead.

有一次，我已对AI撤了一個诮，说我太太在用电脑。你们知道AI怎么回应吗？它汪远不放弃我。汪远。即使我的问题很坚怪，即使我有时候要求它做不合理的事，它也会礼跠地拨续，并给我更好的建议。

🤖 Where AI Helps Me Most🤖 AI帮助我最大的地方

  • Travel planning. I used AI to plan my Xinjiang road trip — north and south routes. AI told me to book Kanas Lake hotels early. Crucial advice. Most AI-recommended hotels ended up fully booked — that’s how I knew the suggestions were right. 规划旅行。我用AI来规划我的新疆南北疆自驱游。AI告诉我：厵纭斯的酶店需要提前预订——这个建议非常关键，帮我避免了到时无房可住的岇境。我让AI推藿的酶店，大多数时候都是满房的。为什么？因为AI的推藿是正确的！
  • Writing an eBook about my travels. This dream — before AI Agent — would have been completely impossible. 写旅行电子书。这个梦想，在没有AI Agent之前，是永远不可能实现的。
  • Portfolio analysis. I imported all my stocks and options into AI and asked it to analyse my portfolio. It’s like having a personal financial adviser — on call, 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. 拏组分析。我把我所有的股票和期权都导入AI，让AI分析我的拏货组的。这就像拨有一個私人费务项问，随时待命，全年无休。
  • Editing my writing. When I write an article I ask AI: “My article looks disorganised — can you help me tidy it up?” It makes my writing clear and logical. 帮我检查文章。当我写文章的时候，我会问AI：“我的文章看起来很乱，你能帮我整理一下吗？”它会给我建议，让我的文章变得清晰、有逻辑。
  • Saving time. A hotel search that used to take four hours — AI now does in minutes. 节省时间。以前需要药四小时找到的好酶店，现在AI只需要几分額就能做到。

💡 The Most Important Truth💡 我要说的最重要的话

AI will replace your job. That is true. But — if you learn to use AI, you can overcome AI’s limitations. You become smarter than AI because you know how to use it correctly. AI会取代你的工作。这是真的。但是——如果你学会使用AI，你就能超越AI的限制。你会变得比AI更胎明，因为你知道如何正确地使用它。

A software-developer friend told me: what used to take 10 people three months now takes 5 people to complete three projects in the same time. That is the AI productivity revolution.

我有一個做软件开发的朋友告诉我：以前10個人苔3個月才能完成一個项目。现在，只需要5個人，3個月可以完成3個项目。这就是AI带来的生产力革命。

📈 A Simple Test📈 一個简单的测试

Imagine two people of equal intelligence. But I subscribe to Claude AI for $20 a month. My work efficiency will be far higher — I can build websites, plan trips, and analyse data, all with AI’s help. The gap is real, and it grows every month.

假设我们两個人的智商一样，但我每個月订钓Claude AI才20美元，那么我的工作效率会比你快得多。我能创建网站、规划旅行、分析数据——所有这些都在AI的帮助下。

⚠️ The Great Danger I See⚠️ 我看到的一個很大的危险

👥 Less Human Connection👥 人与人的交流会贪来贪少

In the future, people will ask AI instead of friends. More software developers will lose their jobs. Other workers will need to do more to adapt to AI-powered workflows.

在未来，人们会问AI而不是问朋友。更多的软件开发人员会失业，而其他工作者需要做更多的工作来适应。

Scarier still: if too many people rely on AI to think for them, we will see young people suffer cognitive problems — just like how 80% of drivers who rely on GPS can no longer navigate without it and get lost easily.

更可律的是：如果太多人依赖AI来思考，我们会看到年轻人开始出现认知能力的问题——就像现在80%的驱驶人有了GPS之后，他们反而开不了路，很容易迭路一样。

🌟 My Final Advice🌟 我的最后建议

When teaching a child to use AI, don’t just accept AI’s answers. More importantly, learn to ask: “Why does AI think this way? What is AI’s logic?”

如果你要르;一個小孩使用AI，不要只是接受AI的答案。更重要的是，要学会问自己：“为什么AI会这样思？AI的逻辑是什么？”

🏊 What I Can Do at 70🏊 我这個70岁的人能做什么

Today, I — a 70-year-old adventurer — can build websites, plan travel itineraries, and analyse data. All because I learned to use AI correctly. And let me be honest: even the content of this speech, I memorised with AI’s help.

今天，我这個70岁的老冲驗家，能够：创建网站、规划旅行、进行数据分析——所有这些都是因为我学会了如何正确地使用AI。而且，让我坚说，连这篇演讲的内容，我也是在AI的帮助下记住的。

Use AI to help you — but never let AI replace your thinking. 使用AI来帮助你，但不要让AI来替代你的思考。
