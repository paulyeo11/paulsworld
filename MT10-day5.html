Hunting for a free youth performance寻找一场免费的青年表演
Started the morning with an idea — go find a free performance by young people, the kind of local energy worth catching on a Sunday. The nearest sure things turned out to be weeks away: Haneda Matsuri (24–26 Jul, taiko and mikoshi near Ōtorii Station) and Kamata's own 大蒲田祭 street festival, usually early August. Nothing pinned down for today itself — so the search became its own small adventure.早上带着一个念头出发 — 找一场年轻人的免费表演，周日里值得一看的那种本地活力。结果发现最确定的两个都还要再等几周：羽田祭（7月24-26日，在大鳥居站附近有太鼓和神轿）和蒲田本地的大蒲田祭街头庆典，通常在8月初举行。今天本身没有确定的活动 — 于是这场寻找本身变成了一次小小的冒险。
🚶
From Keikyu-Kamata to JR Kamata, then on to Aprico Hall从京急蒲田到JR蒲田，再走向Aprico Hall
Walked over from Keikyu-Kamata Station to JR Kamata Station, checking event listings the whole way. Ota City Aprico Hall — just a couple of minutes from the JR station's east exit — had something on its calendar for today, though the online listing didn't say what. Decided the fastest way to find out was to just walk over and check the board in person. Currently on the way there.从京急蒲田站走到JR蒲田站，一路查看活动信息。大田区民ホール・アプリコ——离JR站东口只有几分钟路程——日历上显示今天有安排，但网上没说是什么。决定最快的办法就是直接走过去，现场看公告板。目前正在前往的路上。
