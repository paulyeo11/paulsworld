✅ Screening Criteria✅ 筛选条件

  • Market Cap市值> $10B
  • Price股价> $10
  • Average Volume平均成交量> 500K
  • 200-Day SMA200日均线Price above SMA200股价高于SMA200
  • 50-Day SMA50日均线Price below SMA50股价低于SMA50
  • Chart Pattern图表形态Channel通道

💡 What This Setup Means💡 这个组合代表什么

  • Above the 200-day SMA = the long-term trend is still up. Below the 50-day SMA = the stock has pulled back short-term. Together, this is a classic "uptrend + dip" setup, and the Channel pattern filter narrows it further to stocks moving in an orderly, range-bound way rather than crashing.股价高于200日均线代表长期趋势仍向上；低于50日均线代表短期出现回调。两者结合是经典的"上升趋势中回调"形态，再加上通道形态筛选，可进一步锁定走势有序、区间震荡而非暴跌的股票。
