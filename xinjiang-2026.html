🌤️ Weather Guide — September 2026
⚠️ Big Temperature Swing! Xinjiang September = start in jacket, strip to t-shirt by noon, jacket again at sunset. Day/night difference can be 15–20°C. Always carry a warm layer — especially at Kanas Lake & Hemu Village. Strong UV — wear sunscreen & cap even on cool days.
