Why It's on My Watchlist为何在我的观察清单上

  • OpenAI partner — Copilot is baked into Windows, Office and Azure, giving Microsoft a direct AI monetisation path other mega-caps don't have as cleanly.OpenAI合作伙伴 — Copilot已深度整合进Windows、Office和Azure，为微软提供了其他科技巨头不容易复制的AI变现路径。
  • Highly diversified revenue — cloud, software subscriptions, gaming and search all contribute, so no single product failure sinks the stock.收入高度多元化 — 云、软件订阅、游戏与搜索都有贡献，单一产品失败不会拖垮整只股票。
  • Dividend payer with a long track record of raises — steadier than pure-growth AI names.长期稳定派息并持续加息 — 比纯成长型AI股票更稳健。
  • Usually range-bound between earnings — one reason it screens well for Iron Condor premium-selling (see T21).财报之间通常区间震荡 — 这也是它在T21筛选中适合铁鹰卖权策略的原因之一。
