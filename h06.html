⚠️ Two Things to Follow Up ⚠️ 两项需要跟进的指标

1. Anaemia — Low Haemoglobin (Most Important) 1. 贫血 — 血红蛋白偏低（最重要）

Joanne's haemoglobin is 10.2 g/dL, which is below the normal range of 11.5–16.5 for women. Several related markers are also flagged, all pointing to the same issue. 阿娟的血红蛋白为 10.2 g/dL，低于女性正常范围11.5–16.5。多项相关指标也显示异常，均指向同一问题。

Blood Film finding (from the lab report) 血液涂片结果（来自化验报告） "Dyserythropoietic changes present. Suggestive of Iron Deficiency and/or Thalassaemia Trait."

What this means in plain language: The red blood cells are smaller than normal (low MCV = 61) and carry less haemoglobin than they should. The lab's blood film examination confirms this pattern matches either iron deficiency or thalassaemia trait — or possibly both. 通俗解释： 红细胞比正常的小（MCV偏低 = 61），携带的血红蛋白量也不足。化验室的血液涂片检查确认这种模式符合缺铁性贫血或地中海贫血遗传特性——或两者兼有。

What the doctor should check next 医生接下来应检查的项目 Ask Dr Liew to order Serum Ferritin + Iron Studies. This simple blood test will show whether Joanne's iron stores are low (iron deficiency) or normal (suggesting thalassaemia trait instead). The two conditions look similar but have very different treatments. 请刘医生开血清铁蛋白 + 铁代谢检查。这项简单的血液检查可以判断阿娟的铁储存量是否不足（缺铁性贫血），或是否正常（则可能是地中海贫血遗传特性）。两种情况看似相似，但治疗方法截然不同。
  • Iron Deficiency Anaemia: Treatable — iron supplements + eat more iron-rich foods (spinach, red meat, lentils, tofu) 缺铁性贫血： 可治疗 — 补充铁剂 + 多吃富含铁质的食物（菠菜、红肉、扁豆、豆腐）
  • Thalassaemia Minor (Trait): No treatment needed — it's a genetic characteristic, not a disease. But important to know for family planning (children may inherit it) 轻型地中海贫血（遗传特性）： 无需治疗 — 这是遗传特征，不是疾病。但了解这一点对家庭规划很重要（子女可能遗传）
Symptoms to watch for anaemia 贫血需关注的症状 Unusual fatigue · Shortness of breath doing normal activities · Dizziness or lightheadedness · Pale skin or pale lower eyelids · Heart pounding or racing 异常疲劳 · 日常活动时气短 · 头晕或头重脚轻 · 皮肤或下眼睑苍白 · 心跳加速

2. Gamma GT — Mildly Elevated Liver Enzyme 2. 谷氨酰转肽酶（GGT）— 肝酶轻微偏高

Gamma GT is 53 U/L against a normal range of <40 U/L. This is a mild elevation. All other liver enzymes (ALT, AST, ALP, bilirubin, albumin) are normal. 谷氨酰转肽酶为 53 U/L，正常范围为<40 U/L，属于轻微偏高。其他所有肝脏指标（谷丙转氨酶、谷草转氨酶、碱性磷酸酶、胆红素、白蛋白）均正常。

Common causes of mild Gamma GT elevation 谷氨酰转肽酶轻微偏高的常见原因 Medication side effects · Fatty liver (NAFLD) · Alcohol · Recent illness · Even heavy exercise. Because all other liver markers are normal, this is not urgent — but worth mentioning to Dr Liew so he can monitor it. 药物副作用 · 脂肪肝（非酒精性） · 饮酒 · 近期生病 · 甚至剧烈运动。由于其他所有肝脏指标正常，这并不紧急——但值得告知刘医生，以便持续监测。

  • Continue current lifestyle — blood sugar, kidneys, cholesterol, thyroid all look great 继续现有的生活方式 — 血糖、肾功能、胆固醇、甲状腺各项指标都非常好
