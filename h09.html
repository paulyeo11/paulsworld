Notes from Dr. William Li's video on angiogenesis, inflammation, and the foods that help starve cancer before it starts. 摘自威廉·李医生（Dr. William Li）关于血管生成、炎症以及能在癌症萌芽前"饿死"它的食物的视频笔记。

Dr. William Li explains that abnormal cells form in the body every single day — but the body has powerful, natural systems to stop them from ever becoming cancer. Diet is one of the main levers we can pull to keep those defenses strong. 威廉·李医生指出，体内每天都会产生异常细胞 — 但身体拥有强大的自然防御系统，能阻止它们发展成癌症。饮食正是我们能主动强化这些防御力的关键手段之一。

DNA Repair & DefenseDNA修复与防御

1:34

The body makes roughly 10,000 DNA mistakes every 24 hours. Most are harmless, and the body has repair crews working around the clock to fix them. 身体每24小时会产生约10,000个DNA错误。大多数无害，身体有全天候的"修复队"负责纠正它们。

2:09

Two key defenses keep microscopic abnormalities from turning into cancer: the immune system, which hunts down rogue cells, and anti-angiogenesis — the body's ability to stop a tumor from growing its own blood supply. 两大关键防线能阻止微小异常发展成癌症：一是免疫系统，负责搜寻并清除异常细胞；二是抗血管生成能力 — 身体阻止肿瘤长出自己血管供应的能力。

Cancer is a Seed, Inflammation is the Soil癌症是"种子"，炎症是"土壤"

10:19

Dr. Li's analogy: cancer cells are like a seed that can lie dormant for years — harmless on its own. Chronic inflammation is the soil that can "fertilize" that seed and let it start growing. 李医生的比喻：癌细胞就像一颗种子，可以多年潜伏不动 — 本身并无害。而慢性炎症就是"土壤"，会为这颗种子"施肥"，让它开始生长。

11:42 – 12:30

Triggers like sunburns and excessive alcohol cause chronic inflammation, releasing growth factors that can wake up dormant, otherwise harmless cancer cells. 像晒伤和过量饮酒这类诱因会造成慢性炎症，释放生长因子，可能唤醒原本无害的休眠癌细胞。

Cancer-Fighting Foods抗癌食物

🌱Soy大豆
Contrary to old myths, soy is not something to avoid. It contains genistein, which helps starve cancer by cutting off its blood supply — and it actually blocks human estrogen in estrogen-responsive cancers rather than feeding them (5:34). 与旧观念相反，大豆并非需要避开的食物。它含有大豆异黄酮（genistein），能切断癌细胞的血液供应，从而"饿死"它；对于雌激素敏感型癌症，它实际上是阻断人体雌激素，而不是助长它（5:34）。
Shanghai Breast Cancer Study: 30% lower mortality (5:55) 上海乳腺癌研究：死亡率降低30%（5:55）
🍅Tomatoes & Olive Oil番茄与橄榄油
Lycopene in cooked tomatoes acts as a cancer-starving agent (7:16). Pairing tomatoes with extra virgin olive oil improves how well the body absorbs this compound (8:46). 煮熟番茄中的番茄红素能"饿死"癌细胞（7:16）。搭配特级初榨橄榄油食用，能提高身体对该成分的吸收率（8:46）。
🍎Apples & Berries苹果与莓果
Chlorogenic acid in apples and anthocyanins in colorful berries are powerful bioactives that reduce inflammation, protect DNA, and help starve cancer cells (8:51 – 10:00). 苹果中的绿原酸与色彩鲜艳莓果中的花青素是强效的生物活性物质，能减少炎症、保护DNA，并帮助"饿死"癌细胞（8:51 – 10:00）。
