Three storeys, one skylight — go higher in God, from grace to love, from the Son to the Father.

🌅

Morning Nourishment

Gen. 6:16

You shall make an opening for light for the ark; and you shall put the entrance of the ark in its side; you shall make it with lower, second, and third stories.

Eph. 2:18

For through Him we both have access in one Spirit unto the Father.

Acts 26:19

Therefore, King Agrippa, I was not disobedient to the heavenly vision.

The ark had three stories — lower, second and third. These three stories signify the Triune God. The Spirit is the first story — He comes to us first, finds us, and draws us in. The Son is the second story — the Spirit brings us to Him. The Father is the third story — the Son brings us to the Father.

Think of it like a three-storey house. The Spirit is the entry level. The Son is the middle floor. The Father is the upper floor — the most intimate, the most mysterious. Only those most intimately related to the owner are brought to the third floor to see the hidden treasures.

There was only one opening in the ark — a skylight at the top. Light came from above, not from the north, south, east or west. This means there is only one revelation, one vision. The higher you go in your experience of God, the more light you have.

📖 Today's Reading

John's Gospel brings us to the Son and gives us grace. John's first Epistle brings us to the Father and gives us love. Love is higher than grace. Light is higher than truth. We must not stay at the entry level — we must go on from the Spirit to the Son, and from the Son to the Father.

The degree of light you have shows which storey you are living in. Some Christians are on fire — fervent and enthusiastic — but they are not in much light. Others are quiet, but their presence makes everything clear. When you sit with someone full of God, all darkness vanishes. It is noontime.

In God's economy, there is only one opening and only one light. We should not follow different teachers pulling in different directions. The light comes from above — from the heavenly vision. Paul said he was not disobedient to the heavenly vision. Are we?

Lord, don't let me stay in the first storey. Bring me higher — from the Spirit to the Son, and from the Son to the Father. Let Your light shine on me today.

Big Idea: Three storeys, one skylight — go higher in God, from grace to love, from the Son to the Father.
1
🏠
3 storeys = Father, Son, Spirit

The Spirit draws you in at the entry level. The Son is the middle floor. The Father — the most intimate — is the top. Go higher!

2
☀️
Light shows how high you are

The more light you carry, the higher your story. Some believers are fervent but dark. Others are quiet but their presence is like noontime.

3
🪟
One opening — from above

Only one skylight, only one source of light. God's revelation comes from above — not from man's cleverness or human teaching.

4
❤️
Go on from grace to love

Grace is in the Son. Love is in the Father. Truth is good — light is better. Don't stop at the Son; go on to the Father.

5
👁️
Don't disobey the heavenly vision

Paul was not disobedient to the vision God gave him. When God shows you something, obey it — even if no one else understands.
