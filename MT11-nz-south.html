🗺️ Deep South Route Map🗺️ 极南路线地图

A focused loop through New Zealand's far south — the wildest, least-crowded corner. From Queenstown down the Southern Scenic Route to the very tip at Bluff, across to Stewart Island, then along the rugged Catlins coast to Dunedin. Penguins, sea lions, kiwi, fjords and lighthouses.深入新西兰极南的专属环线 —— 最狂野、人最少的一角。从皇后镇沿南部观景公路南下，直抵最南端的布拉夫，渡海前往斯图尔特岛，再沿崎岖的卡特林斯海岸抵达但尼丁。企鹅、海狮、几维鸟、峡湾与灯塔。

💡 Deep South Tips💡 极南贴士

⛴
Stewart Island = ferry, no car斯图尔特岛＝渡轮，不带车
The ferry from Bluff is foot-passenger only — park the car at Bluff and travel light. Book ferry & lodge ahead; it's small.布拉夫的渡轮仅限步行乘客 —— 把车停在布拉夫、轻装前往。渡轮与住宿都要提前订，岛很小。
🐧
Keep your distance from wildlife与野生动物保持距离
Yellow-eyed penguins and sea lions are rare and easily spooked — stay well back, stay quiet, no flash. Dawn/dusk are best.黄眼企鹅和海狮稀有且易受惊 —— 请远远观看、保持安静、不用闪光灯。清晨与黄昏最佳。
🌦️
The south is wild & changeable南部狂野多变
Four seasons in a day, strong coastal wind. Pack warm, waterproof layers even in spring — and allow flexible days for weather.一天四季、海风强劲。即使春天也要带保暖防水衣物 —— 并预留弹性天数应对天气。
