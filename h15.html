h15: 5 Foods That Calm Inflammationh15：5种平息炎症的食物

Key points from Dr. William Li's video — five science-backed foods that help turn down chronic inflammation. 摘自 William Li 博士视频的重点 — 五种有科学依据、能帮助降低慢性炎症的食物。
▶ Watch Video

Dr. William Li explains that chronic inflammation is an overactive immune response that quietly drives aging and disease. These five foods help support the body's own defense systems and turn that inflammation down. William Li 博士指出，慢性炎症是一种过度活跃的免疫反应，会悄悄加速衰老、诱发疾病。以下五种食物能帮助支持身体自身的防御系统，把炎症"调低"。

The 5 Anti-Inflammatory Foods五种抗炎食物

1
Vitamin C–Rich Foods富含维生素C的食物
0:12–2:45

Oranges, strawberries, broccoli and cherry tomatoes. A study on women with lupus (Miyagi Prefecture) found that a high vitamin C intake — about 154 mg/day — significantly reduced inflammatory flares. 橙子、草莓、西兰花和樱桃番茄。一项针对狼疮女性患者（宫城县）的研究发现，高维生素C摄入 — 约每天154毫克 — 能显著减少炎症发作。

  • Boosts T-reg (regulatory) cells — the "volume switch" that turns down the immune system's attack cells提升 T-reg（调节性T）细胞 — 相当于把免疫系统攻击细胞"调小声"的音量开关
  • Target from the study: about 154 mg vitamin C per day研究中的目标：每天约154毫克维生素C
2
Green Tea绿茶
2:52–4:58

Rich in the polyphenol EGCG (epigallocatechin gallate), which also increases T-reg cells and lowers inflammatory markers in the blood. 富含多酚 EGCG（表没食子儿茶素没食子酸酯），同样能增加 T-reg 细胞，并降低血液中的炎症指标。

  • Five cups a day cut lupus activity by 50% and improved quality of life in studies研究显示：每天五杯可将狼疮活动度降低50%，并改善生活质量
3
Matcha抹茶
5:04–6:59

A concentrated, powdered form of green tea. Because you consume the whole leaf, you get even more EGCG plus dietary fiber that feeds your gut microbiome. 绿茶的浓缩粉状形式。由于你吃下的是整片茶叶，能摄取更多 EGCG，还有滋养肠道菌群的膳食纤维。

  • A healthy microbiome makes short-chain fatty acids (acetate, propionate, butyrate)健康的肠道菌群会产生短链脂肪酸（乙酸、丙酸、丁酸）
  • Those fatty acids further lower inflammation in the bloodstream这些脂肪酸能进一步降低血液中的炎症
4
Oolong Tea乌龙茶
7:01–7:15

A slightly fermented (oxidized) tea that also carries beneficial anti-inflammatory polyphenols. 一种轻度发酵（氧化）的茶，同样含有有益的抗炎多酚。

5
Black & Dark Teas红茶与黑茶
7:16–7:30

English Breakfast, Earl Grey, and the ultra-fermented Pu-erh all contain anti-inflammatory catechins. 英式早餐茶、伯爵茶，以及深度发酵的普洱茶，都含有抗炎的儿茶素。

Key Health Tips健康小贴士

🍵 How You Brew Matters🍵 冲泡方式很重要

Avoid pyramid-shaped tea bags often made of nylon (plastic) — they can release microplastics into hot water. Choose whole loose leaves, paper tea bags, or pure matcha powder instead. (7:36–8:42) 避免常用尼龙（塑料）制成的三角金字塔茶包 — 它们会在热水中释放微塑料。改用整片散茶、纸质茶包，或纯抹茶粉。（7:36–8:42）

🌿 Feed Your Gut with Fiber🌿 用纤维滋养肠道

Fiber-rich foods foster a healthy gut microbiome — a crucial part of the body's systemic anti-inflammatory defense. (6:34–7:00) 富含纤维的食物能培养健康的肠道菌群 — 这是身体全身性抗炎防御的关键一环。（6:34–7:00）

Not Medical Advice非医疗建议

This is a summary of Dr. William Li's video for personal interest, not medical advice. If you have lupus, an autoimmune condition, or take regular medication, check with your doctor before making big diet changes. 这是 William Li 博士视频的个人兴趣摘要，并非医疗建议。若你患有狼疮、自身免疫疾病或长期服药，在大幅调整饮食前请先咨询医生。
