My Travel

by Paul Yeo

A personal travel memoir capturing decades of adventures, discoveries, and life lessons gathered from journeys across Asia, Europe, and beyond.

From hiking trails in Singapore to exploring the coasts of Portugal, from the mountains of Xinjiang to the streets of Hong Kong — every chapter is a story worth telling.

✈️ Travel Memoir 📖 First Book 🌏 Asia & Europe 2026

The Achievement

At 70 years old, I accomplished something I once thought might remain a dream: I completed and published my first book. My Travel is a memoir born from a lifetime of adventures — from spontaneous detours to carefully planned expeditions — and the human connections made along the way.

"A travel memoir is not just about the places you visit — it is about the person you become through those journeys."

This book is a record of real experiences: getting lost in MacRitchie Reservoir, losing my iPhone in Portugal, self-driving through the golden forests of Xinjiang, and countless quieter moments shared with Joanne and dear friends around the world.

The Journey of Writing

Writing this book required patience to organise decades of memories, discipline to sit down and write consistently, and courage to share personal stories with the world. Using modern tools — including AI assistance — helped structure my thoughts and refine my prose, proving that age and technology can work beautifully together.

The writing process itself became a second adventure. Revisiting old stories revealed new insights I had not noticed at the time. In many ways, writing gave me a chance to travel those roads again.

With Gratitude

This book would not exist without my wife Joanne, whose encouragement kept me going through every chapter. Thank you to my Toastmasters community for sharpening my voice, and to every travel companion, stranger-turned-friend, and local guide who made each journey unforgettable.

Retirement is not an ending — it is the beginning of your most creative chapter yet.
