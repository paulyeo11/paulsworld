🔒 Privacy First

Your voice never
leaves your device.

Most dictation tools send your audio to a cloud server, store it in a database, and use it to train their models. Glaido does none of that. Everything runs locally on your machine — offline, always.

This isn't just a privacy policy promise. It's how the product is built at the architecture level. There is no server to send your audio to. Period.

  • Audio processed on your device, not in the cloud
  • No account required — works without sign-in
  • No usage data or transcripts stored anywhere
  • Works fully offline, no internet connection needed
  • GDPR / CCPA compliant by design — not just policy
