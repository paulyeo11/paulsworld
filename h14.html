A gum-care mouth rinse and an eye wash I picked up on this Japan trip — simple habits for gum health and tired eyes. 这趟日本行带回来的两款好物 — 一款牙龈护理漱口水、一款洗眼液，帮助照顾牙龈健康和疲劳的双眼。

At 70, small daily habits matter more than big fixes. These two products, both bought at a Japanese pharmacy, have become part of my morning and evening routine. 70岁之后，日常的小习惯比大动作的"修复"更重要。这两款产品都是在日本药妆店买的，如今已成为我早晚例行护理的一部分。
