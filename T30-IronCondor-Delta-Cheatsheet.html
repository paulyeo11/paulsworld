First — What Delta Actually Means先搞懂 — Delta 到底是什么

Delta ≈ the probability the option finishes in-the-money. A put at −0.25 delta has roughly a 25% chance of being ITM at expiry — so about a 75% chance it expires worthless and you keep the premium.Delta ≈ 期权到期时变为价内（ITM）的概率。一个 −0.25 delta 的认沽，到期时变为价内的概率约 25%——也就是约 75% 的概率归零，你保留全部权利金。

Put deltas are negative (−0.25), call deltas positive (+0.25). "Delta 25" just means the strike whose delta reads 0.25 (ignore the sign). Lower delta = further out-of-the-money = safer but less premium.认沽 delta 为负（−0.25），认购 delta 为正（+0.25）。"Delta 25"就是 delta 读数为 0.25 的那个行使价（不看正负号）。delta 越低 = 越价外 = 越安全但权利金越少。

⚖️ The Golden Rule — Keep It Balanced⚖️ 黄金法则 — 保持平衡

Whatever delta you sell the put at, sell the call at the same delta. This centres the condor around the current price so neither side is favoured.无论你把认沽卖在哪个 delta，就把认购卖在相同的 delta。这样铁鹰会以现价为中心，两侧不偏不倚。

🤷 If You Can't Find Exactly 0.25🤷 如果找不到刚好 0.25

Never force it — take the nearest delta. Strikes only come in fixed steps (e.g. $1 apart), so you rarely land on an exact number. Pick the strike whose delta is closest to your target.不要勉强——取最接近的 delta。行使价只有固定间距（例如相差 $1），所以很难刚好命中某个数值。挑选 delta 最接近目标的那个行使价即可。

Example from Paul's QQQ chain (Aug 28 expiry): the puts read −0.247 at strike 669 and −0.251 at strike 670. Both are effectively "delta 25" — either one works.以 Paul 的 QQQ 期权链为例（8 月 28 日到期）：认沽在行使价 669 读数 −0.247，在 670 读数 −0.251。两者实际上都是"delta 25"——任选其一都行。

📱 How to Show Delta in IBKR (Mobile)📱 如何在 IBKR（手机版）显示 Delta

  1. Open the stock → Options tab (the CALLS · STRIKE · PUTS chain).打开股票 → Options 标签（CALLS · STRIKE · PUTS 期权链）。
  2. Tap the blue sliders icon on the left, next to the expiry-date row.点击左侧到期日期一行旁边的蓝色滑块图标。
  3. In Options Settings, scroll down and tap Manage Columns.在 Options Settings 里向下滑，点击 Manage Columns。
  4. Tap the blue ⊕ next to Delta → back → Done.点击 Delta 旁边的蓝色 ⊕ → 返回 → Done。
  5. Back on the chain, scroll the puts down (strikes below the price) and find the one closest to −0.25.回到期权链，向下滑动认沽（低于现价的行使价），找到最接近 −0.25 的那一个。

Note: Delta is not in the "Manage Metrics" menu (that's for the quote/watchlist stats) — it's inside the option chain's own Manage Columns.注意：Delta 不在"Manage Metrics"菜单里（那是行情/自选股统计用的）——它在期权链自己的 Manage Columns 里。
