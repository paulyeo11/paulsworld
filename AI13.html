First: What’s the Difference Between Claude Code and Claude Cowork?

This confused me at first. I kept hearing both names. Here is how I now understand them:

💻 Claude Code

A tool you run in a terminal (command line). It reads your files, writes code, fixes bugs, and builds things on your computer. It works with your actual project files.

🤝 Claude Cowork

A tool you use in the browser or app. It works alongside you in your daily tasks — writing, research, presentations, reports, and personal productivity. Think of it as your AI co-worker.

Simple way to remember: Claude Code = for building software. Claude Cowork = for getting everyday work done.

· · ·

The 3-Step Framework: How to Get Better Results

The video taught a simple framework. Most people give Claude a vague request and get a vague result. If you follow these three steps, the output is much better:

The OCF Framework

1
Outcome Tell Claude exactly what you want to achieve. Not “write me a report” — but “write a 1-page report summarising my investment performance this month.”
2
Context Give Claude the background it needs. Who is this for? What do you already know? What has been tried before?
3
Format Tell Claude how you want the answer. A bullet list? A table? A short paragraph? An HTML page? The clearer you are, the better the result.

I tried this myself. Before I used OCF, I would say: “Summarise this article.” After OCF, I say: “Summarise this article in 5 bullet points for a 70-year-old reader who is new to AI, using simple English.” The difference in quality is enormous.

3 Key Features to Know

What Makes Cowork Powerful

🛠️
Skills Pre-built abilities Claude can use to complete specific tasks — like analysing data, writing in your style, or following a workflow you define.
🔗
Connectors Claude can connect to your tools — Google Drive, email, calendars, and more — so it can work with your real data, not just hypothetical examples.
