What Changed发生了什么变化

On 23 Jul earlier in the day, QQQ was at $705.35 — Put Variance 3.59%, ⚠️ Tight but not urgent. By evening, QQQ had dropped to $693.94 (−$11.41 / −1.62% that day), pulling both of my own warning checks into the red at once:7月23日白天，QQQ为$705.35，认沽差幅3.59%——⚠️偏紧但尚不紧急。到晚间，QQQ已跌至$693.94（当日下跌$11.41 / -1.62%），我自订的两个预警同时进入红色区域：

Put Variance (A−B)/A = (693.94−680)/693.94 = 2.0% → 🔴 Very Tight (below 3%)
Nearest cut-loss (A−low)/A = (693.94−678)/693.94 = 2.3% → 🔴 danger zone (below 3%)

Only $15.94 (2.3%) of room was left before the hard $678 cut-loss — and that gap had shrunk fast, confirmed by the option leg prices themselves: the short 680P and long 675P had both jumped +31–32% since entry, while the short 745C and long 750C had both dropped −38% to −43%. That's a real directional move, not just time decay.距$678硬止损位只剩$15.94（2.3%）的空间——而且这个空间正在快速收窄，从期权各腿价格本身也能证实：卖出的680P与买入的675P自建仓以来均上涨31–32%，而卖出的745C与买入的750C均下跌38%至43%。这是真实的方向性走势，不只是时间衰减。

The Tension — and the Call矛盾之处——以及最终判断

Unrealized profit at that point was only $23–24 — about 9% of the $259 max credit, far short of the usual 50%-of-credit take-profit target (~$130). Normally that argues for holding.当时未实现盈利仅$23–24——约为$259最大权利金的9%，远低于通常50%权利金（约$130）的获利了结目标。一般来说这代表应该续持。

But per my own standing rule — check Nearest cut-loss first, it covers both directions — a red-zone reading takes priority over the profit target. Holding for another $100+ of profit risked the full $241 max loss for a shrinking reward, with price only one more bad day from the hard stop.但根据我自己订下的准则——先看「最近止损」，它同时涵盖两侧方向——红色危险区的信号应优先于获利目标。为了再赚$100多而续持，等于是用$241的最大亏损去搏一个正在缩小的回报，而股价只差一个坏交易日就会触及硬止损。

✅ Take the small win now, rather than risk the cap for a bigger one that may not arrive.先拿下这笔小额盈利，而不是冒着触及最大亏损的风险去赌一个可能等不到的更大回报。

How I Placed the Closing Order我如何下平仓单

1
Tap Buy, not Sell点「Buy」，不是「Sell」
This condor was opened for a credit (sold to open, avg price -2.56). Closing it means buying it back — so on the position screen, the correct button is Buy. Sell would open a brand-new short position, which is the opposite of what's needed.这个铁鹰是以权利金方式建仓的（卖出开仓，均价-2.56）。平仓即买回——所以在持仓页面上，正确的按钮是Buy。按「Sell」会开出一个全新的卖空仓位，方向正好相反。
2
Leave the pre-filled limit price保留预填的限价
The order ticket auto-filled Limit Price -2.19 — the current Ask, with real size (9 contracts) behind it. In plain terms: pay about $219 to close. That's a marketable price likely to fill fast, and it's better than the ~$232 fair-value estimate from the position snapshot — meaning the closing profit ends up a bit higher than first estimated. The "-2.19 / CREDIT" label is just the app's sign convention for this field, not a claim that money is being received.订单自动填入限价-2.19——即当前卖价（Ask），且有实际挂单量（9口合约）支撑。简单说：平仓约需支付$219。这是一个可成交的价位，大概率能快速成交，而且比持仓快照估算的约$232公允价值更划算——代表最终获利会比最初估算的略高一些。「-2.19 / CREDIT」字样只是App对这个栏位的正负号显示惯例，不代表会收到这笔钱。
3
Slide to Buy滑动「Slide to Buy」
Given the wide 17% spread on this combo and the cushion already in the danger zone, speed mattered more than shaving a few more cents — so the order was sent at the marketable Ask rather than walking the price toward mid.由于这个组合单价差高达17%，加上缓冲已进入危险区，速度比再省几分钱更重要——因此按可成交的卖价（Ask）下单，而不是往中间价慢慢试。
