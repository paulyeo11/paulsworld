The core rule核心法则
10% ≈ 0.25 delta
Sell the short put and short call 10% away from the current share price on each side. That 10% gap lines up with roughly a 0.25 delta — Bang's shortcut for picking strikes without hunting through the option chain for the exact delta reading.短认沽与短认购都卖在现价上下各10%处。这个10%缺口大约对应 0.25 delta——这是 Bang 的捷径，不必在期权链里逐一寻找精确的 delta 读数。

1️⃣ Short Strikes — the 10% / Delta 25 Rule1️⃣ 短腿行使价——10%／Delta25法则

For a 4-leg Iron Condor, the two short legs (short put + short call) are the ones collecting premium and defining your profit zone. Bang's rule: pick strikes that sit 10% below and 10% above the current share price.在四腿铁鹰中，两条短腿（短认沽＋短认购）负责收取权利金并界定盈利区。Bang 的法则：选在现价下方10%与上方10%的行使价。

Example — share price $200示例——现价$200
Short Put ≈ $180  ·  Short Call ≈ $220

This 10% distance is a proxy for 0.25 delta — meaning roughly a 25% chance either strike gets breached before expiry. It's a slightly more aggressive strike selection than the textbook 0.16 delta "1 standard deviation" condor (see T30), trading a bit more risk for more premium.这10%的距离约等同于 0.25 delta——意味着任一行使价在到期前被突破的概率约25%。相比教科书式0.16 delta的"1个标准差"铁鹰（见T30），这属于稍微激进一点的行使价选择，以多一点风险换取更多权利金。

2️⃣ Wings — How Wide to Buy the Protection2️⃣ 翼——保护腿该买多宽

The two long legs (long put + long call) are bought further out to cap your max loss — these are the "wings." Bang teaches a $5 or $10 gap beyond each short strike, picked by strike price / liquidity of the underlying. Paul's default: $5.两条长腿（长认沽＋长认购）买在更外侧，用来限制最大亏损——即"翼"。Bang 教的是在每条短腿之外再留 $5 或 $10 的缺口，具体视标的的行使价／流动性而定。Paul 的默认值：$5。

Paul's 4 legs (share price $200 example)Paul的四条腿（以现价$200为例）
Buy Put $175 · Sell Put $180 · Sell Call $220 · Buy Call $225

Narrower wings ($5) = less capital tied up as max loss, but a slightly lower net credit than wider wings ($10). Wider wings = more premium, but more capital at risk per trade.翼较窄（$5）＝占用的最大亏损资金较少，但净收权利金也略低于较宽的翼（$10）。翼较宽＝权利金更多，但每笔交易承担的风险资金也更多。

3️⃣ The Monthly Profit Target3️⃣ 每月盈利目标

Bang's benchmark: if the share price stays within that 10% band for the roughly one-month life of the trade, expect a profit of about $250 per Iron Condor.Bang 的基准：若股价在该笔交易约一个月的存续期内，始终维持在10%区间之内，预期每个铁鹰约能获利 $250。

4️⃣ The DTE 15 Early-Exit Rule4️⃣ DTE15提早离场法则

If, after DTE 15 (15 days left to expiry), the price suddenly drops more than 10%, close all 4 legs immediately — don't wait for expiry. Bang's lesson: closing early here loses less than closing at the end of DTE.如果在DTE15之后（距到期还剩15天），股价突然下跌超过10%，应立即平掉全部4条腿——不要等到到期。Bang 的教训是：此时提早平仓的亏损会比等到DTE结束才平仓来得少。

Why: the wing width caps your worst case, but you only pay that full amount if the breach is still there at expiry. Closing right away at DTE 15 locks in today's smaller loss while there's still time left for the price to recover. Ride it out instead, and that chance of recovery disappears — if the price is still down when expiry arrives, you take the full loss the wing was built to cap. Same breach, but waiting removes your only way out of it.原因：翼宽限制了最坏情况，但只有当突破一直持续到到期日，你才会付出这笔全额亏损。在DTE15时立刻平仓，能在还有时间等待股价回升的情况下，先锁定当下较小的亏损。若选择硬撑到底，这个回升的机会就没有了——如果到期时股价仍未回升，你就要承受翼宽所限定的全额最大亏损。同样是被突破，但等待等于放弃了唯一的脱身机会。

5️⃣ IBKR Commission — Read This Before You Trade5️⃣ IBKR手续费——下单前务必知道

Opening one 4-leg Iron Condor on IBKR costs $2.50 USD. Closing it costs another $2.50 USD. Total round-trip: $5.00 USD per trade.在 IBKR 开一个四腿铁鹰仓位收费 $2.50美元，平仓再收 $2.50美元。每笔交易来回共计 $5.00美元。

Effect on the $250 target对$250目标的影响
$250 − $5 commission = $245 net (~2% of the target)

$5 is small next to a $250 win, but it's not free — factor it in when comparing IC returns against smaller trades or against rolling/adjusting a position (which adds another round of commission each time).相较于$250的盈利，$5看似不多，但并非零成本——在比较铁鹰报酬与较小额交易，或与展期／调整仓位（每次调整都会再产生一轮手续费）时，务必把这笔费用算进去。
