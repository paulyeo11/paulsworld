Tiered vs Fixed — Why It Matters for IG分级式 vs 固定式 — 为何对IG（铁鹰）很重要

IBKR Pro accounts can choose between two commission plans. An Iron Condor (IG) is a 4-leg order, so the per-contract rate gets charged 4× on entry and 4× on exit — small differences in the plan add up fast.IBKR Pro账户可在两种佣金方案中选择。铁鹰（IG）是4腿订单，因此每张合约的费率在开仓与平仓时都要乘以4次——方案之间的微小差异会迅速累积。

Plan方案How it works运作方式
Tiered分级式Per-contract rate drops as monthly volume rises; exchange & regulatory fees passed through at cost (no markup) — usually cheaper for active multi-leg traders每张合约费率随月交易量增加而降低；交易所及监管费按实际成本转嫁（不加价）——对活跃的多腿交易者通常更划算
Fixed固定式One flat all-in rate per contract regardless of volume — simpler, but no volume discount无论交易量多少，每张合约收取统一的全包费率——较简单，但没有交易量折扣

See the fee breakdown in T23: 4-Leg Iron Condor Guide for what a round-trip actually costs.实际来回一趟的费用明细请见 T23：四腿铁鹰交易指南。

Step-by-Step: Check / Switch Your Plan逐步教学：查看／切换你的方案

1
Confirm you're on IBKR Pro确认你使用的是IBKR Pro
Tiered/Fixed only applies to Pro accounts. Open the IBKR app home screen — look for the "IBKR PRO" badge on your profile icon next to your account number.分级式／固定式仅适用于Pro账户。打开IBKR应用主页——在账号旁的头像图标上查找「IBKR PRO」徽章。
2
Open Settings打开「设置」
Tap the hamburger menu → Settings.点击左上角菜单图标 → 设置。
3
Account Settings → All Account Settings账户设置 → 所有账户设置
Tap Account Settings, then the blue "All Account Settings" button — this opens the full Client Portal settings page.点击「账户设置」，再点击蓝色的「所有账户设置」按钮——这会打开完整的客户端后台设置页面。
4
Select the account选择账户
If you have more than one IBKR account, pick the one you trade IG (Iron Condor) in, then tap Continue.如果你有多个IBKR账户，选择你用来交易IG（铁鹰）的那个，然后点击「继续」。
5
Scroll to Account Configuration滚动到「账户配置」
Scroll down past Account Profile/Financial Profile to the "Account Configuration" section.滚动经过「账户资料」／「财务资料」，到达「账户配置」部分。
6
Check "IBKR Pricing Plan"查看「IBKR定价方案」
This line shows your current plan directly — e.g. "IBKR Pro; Futures: Tiered, Stocks: Tiered". If it already says Tiered, you're done. If it says Fixed, tap it to switch to Tiered.这一行会直接显示你目前的方案——例如「IBKR Pro；期货：分级式，股票：分级式」。若已显示Tiered（分级式），即已完成设置。若显示Fixed（固定式），点击即可切换为Tiered。
