📋 My Profit-Take Rules我的止盈规则

  • 1
    Scale out in thirds. At +$10k Open P&L, close 1/3 of the position to lock in real cash. At +$15k, close another 1/3.分批止盈。浮盈达到 +$10,000 时，先平掉三分之一仓位锁定现金。达到 +$15,000 时再平掉三分之一。
  • 2
    Trailing stop on the rest. Once peak profit passes $10k, never let it give back more than 30% from the peak — that's the "Trailing Stop Level" above. If Open P&L drops to that level, close everything.剩余仓位设移动止盈。一旦峰值浮盈超过 $10,000，回撤幅度绝不超过峰值的 30%（即上方的"移动止盈线"）。一旦浮盈跌到该线，全部平仓。
  • 3
    No exceptions for "it might come back." Greed to let a winner run without any stop is exactly what turned +$10k into $0 before.不要因为"可能还会涨回去"而破例。正是这种贪心让获利仓位在没有止损的情况下，从 +$10,000 跌回归零。

🧠 Lesson Learned经验教训

Past cycle: unrealized profit exceeded $10,000, then dropped suddenly to zero — not a slow bleed, a fast drop with no stop in place to catch it. This tracker exists so that next time Open P&L crosses $10k, I actually act (scale out + trailing stop) instead of just watching it. 过往经历：浮动盈利曾超过 $10,000，随后迅速跌回归零 — 不是缓慢回落，而是快速下跌且当时没有设置止损。这个追踪工具的存在，就是为了确保下次浮盈再次突破 $10,000 时，我会真正采取行动（分批止盈 + 移动止盈线），而不是眼睁睁看着它跌回去。
