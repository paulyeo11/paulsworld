h03: The Photo That Inspired My Next Decade

A Single Image That Changed How I See My Future一张改变我对未来看法的图像

The Photo That Started It All引发一切的那张照片

In April 2026, someone took a photo of me. When I saw it, I had a mixed reaction — part of me was surprised, part of me was proud. But mostly, I felt inspired.

I looked at that photo and thought: This is 70. And 70 looks pretty good.

What I Saw in That Photo我在那张照片中看到了什么

I saw a man who had lived 70 years — who had worked hard, raised a family, travelled the world, and was now in the best chapter of his life: retirement.

I also saw possibility. I'm healthy. I'm active. I'm still learning. The next decade doesn't have to be a slow decline — it can be an adventure.

My Promise to Myself我对自己的承诺

💪 My Commitments for My 70s

  • Stay physically active — walk, swim, hike, adventure
  • Keep learning — especially AI and technology
  • Travel to at least 5 new countries before I'm 75
  • Write and share my stories with the world
  • Invest in relationships — family, friends, community
  • Maintain my health numbers — blood pressure, cholesterol, weight

Age Is Just a Number年龄只是一个数字

I used to think 70 was old. Now I know it's just a number. What matters is how you feel, what you do, and who you love. I wake up every morning grateful to be alive and excited about what the day holds.

This photo reminds me of that every time I see it. That's why it inspired my next decade.

"The best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is now." — Chinese Proverb
