AI220: How AI Plans My Daily Driving Route

AI220：AI如何为我规划每日的骑车路线

I told it where I was staying and where I wanted to eat. It came back with a real driving route, a walking leg when a car didn't make sense, and a little icon that moves along the actual roads.

我只是告诉它我住在哪里、想去哪里吃饭。它还我一条真实的骑车路线——当开车没意义时还带上了一段步行路程，并集好兴条图标沿着真实道路移动。

“I never opened a maps app. I just described my day — hotel, pizza, dairy farm, back to the hotel — and told it one leg should be a walk, not a drive. It handled the geocoding, the real roads, and even swapped the moving icon from a car to a person on foot.” “我从未打开过地图应用。我只是描述了我的一天——酒店、抵躱店、牛奶场、再返回酒店——并告诉它其中一段要步行，不要开车。它自己找地址、查真实道路，甚至把移动图标从汽车换成了步行者。”

💡 What I Actually Asked For💡 我到底请求了什么

Planning Niseko Day 7, I had three places in plain English: the Torifito Hotel where I was staying, a rolled-pizza place called Pi-kan Rollpizza, and a dairy farm called Milk Kobo for soft serve afterwards. I just described the day and let AI work out the rest — where each place actually sits on a map, what order made sense, and how to get between them.

在计划二世谷第七天时，我仅用普通话描述了三个地方：我住的托里菲托酒店、叫Pi-kan卷饼抵躱的店，以及之后去吃软雪糖的高槝牛奶场。我只是描述了这一天，其余的交给AI去查——每个地方精确在地图上的位置、哪个顺序更合理、以及怎么在它们之间移动。

Then, once the first version was built, I sent one short line: "Pt B to Pt C — not by driving car, by walking." That was it. No new coordinates, no re-explaining the trip — just a correction in plain language.

第一版束好后，我只发了一句话：“B点到C点——不要开车，改成步行。”就这样。没有重新给坐标，也没有重新解释一遍行程——只是用普通话改了一句。

I never gave a single latitude or longitude. I gave place names and one sentence about how I'd get between two of them. 我从未给过任何经純度坐标。我给的是地点名称，以及关于如何在两位之间移动的一句话。

✅ What AI Figured Out For Me✅ AI为我找出的事

  • Thing 1第一件
    Real Roads, Not Straight Lines. Every leg of the route is fetched from an actual road-routing service, so the line on the map bends through real turns instead of cutting a straight path through fields and rooftops. 真实道路，而非直线。路线的每一段都是从真实的道路导航服务获取的，地图上的线条会随着真实转怨振，而不是积直穿过农田或屋顶的直线。
  • Thing 2第二件
    Switched Modes When I Said So. One sentence — "not by driving car, by walking" — was enough. AI re-routed that leg using a walking-specific road network, changed the moving icon to a person on foot, and recalculated the time using a walking pace instead of a driving speed. 按我的话切换出行方式。只需一句“不要开车，改成步行”。AI就用步行专用的道路网重新计算那一段，把移动图标换成了步行者，并按步行速度而非骑车速度重新算了时间。
  • Thing 3第三件
    Added the Return Trip When I Asked. "Show from Pt C drive to Pt A" added a whole new stop and leg — the drive back to the hotel — without me touching a single coordinate already on the page. 按我的要求加上返程。一句“显示C点骑车返回A点”，就增加了一个全新的站点和路程——骑车返回酒店——而我无需拖动页面上已有的任何坐标。
  • Thing 4第四件
    Fixed What I Couldn't See. When I said the Play button was too small and hidden below the fold on my phone, AI made it a bold gold pill and shrank the map so the button fits on screen without scrolling — no design brief needed beyond "make it more visible." 修好了我看不到的问题。当我说播放按钮太小，在手机上需要滑动才能看到时，AI把它改成了重粗金色臓址，并缩小了地图，让按钮不滑动就能看到——我只说了“让它更明显”，几乎没有其他设计要求。

⚠️ What I Still Checked Myself⚠️ 我仍自己查核的事

The route AI draws is only as good as the address it started from. A couple of stops on Day 7 had coordinates that weren't confirmed by a real mapping service, so the page carries a visible warning on those pins and routes the actual "Directions" button off the address text instead of a guessed pin — never off a location I haven't verified myself.

AI画出的路线，关键人在于一开始的地址是否准确。第七天有几个站点的坐标未经真实地图服务确认，所以页面上会显示明显的警示，并且“导航”按钮使用的是地址文字而非推测的坐标——从不用我自己未确认过的位置。

A Route I Never Drew一条我从未画过的路线

I've planned plenty of driving days by hand — a list of addresses and a rough guess at the order. This is the first time I described a day in a sentence and got back a map that knows the real roads, knows when to put me on foot instead of behind a wheel, and moves on its own.

我以前用手计划过不少骑车行程——一排地址加上大致的顺序。这是我第一次用一句话描述一天，就得到一张知道真实道路、知道何时让我改成步行而非开车、并自己移动的地图。

If a 70-year-old retiree can plan a day out just by talking to it — no maps app, no route-planning software — that says something about how far this technology has come.

如果一位70岁的退休者只需跨它说话，不用地图应用，不用路线规划软件，就能计划好一天的行程，那说明这项技术已经进步到什么程度。
