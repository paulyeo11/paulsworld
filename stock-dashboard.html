📖 What Do These Numbers Mean?

Today's PriceThe current market price of one share. Press "Get Latest Prices" to update.
I Paid Per ShareYour average purchase price per share, from your IB Portfolio Analyst report (exact cost basis).
Unrealised LossHow much you are down on paper. No real money is lost until you actually sell the shares.
Daily Price Swing (1-SD)How much the stock price typically moves up or down each day. Smaller = steadier stock.
Avg Daily Move (ADR)The average dollar amount the price moved from one day to the next, over the past 30 days.
Year Low / Year HighThe cheapest and most expensive this stock has been in the past 12 months. The bar shows where today's price sits.
20% Below StrikeThe price that is exactly 20% below today's share price (current price × 80%). Useful as a put option strike reference or a mental stop-loss — if the stock falls 20%, this is where it lands.
0.16 Delta LevelThe price that is exactly 1 standard deviation below today's price. In options trading, a 0.16 delta put has about a 16% chance of the stock falling to that level. Useful as a downside target or stop-loss reference.
