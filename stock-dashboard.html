📖 What Do These Numbers Mean?

Today's Price The current market price of one share. Updated when you press "Get Latest Prices".
I Paid (Avg Cost) The average price you paid per share, based on your Interactive Brokers report.
Unrealised Gain/Loss How much profit or loss you have on paper right now. No money changes hands until you sell.
Daily Price Swing (1-SD) How much this stock's price typically moves up or down each day. A smaller number means a steadier stock.
Avg Daily Move (ADR) The average dollar amount the stock price moved from one day to the next, over the past 30 days.
52-Week Range The lowest and highest price this stock has been over the past 12 months. The bar shows where today's price sits in that range.
